ResponseWare and Turning Account

A Turning Account may be required to use ResponseWare. A Turning Account is a free account that provides a unique identifier for each user and can be tied to all response devices, including ResponseWare. Instructors and classrooms using TurningPoint Cloudmay require a Turning Account license to register response devices for courses and to receive credit in class. The Turning Account license is available for purchase from the Turning Technologies Student Store.

If the presenter is using TurningPoint Cloud, a Turning Account license is mandatory. If you do not purchase a Turning Account license, your score will show as an asterisk in all TurningPoint Cloud participant reports. Furthermore, your score will export as "0" to an Excel workbook and will not be exported to an LMS.

A Turning Account license may also be required to be able to respond to questions from TurningPoint Cloud.
