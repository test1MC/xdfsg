Messaging the Presenter and the Group

Participants can send questions or comments to the presenter via the Messaging icon. The presenter may enable an option that allows for participants to message the entire group including the presenter.

If you have a message, an alert badge is displayed in the upper right corner.

  1. Tap in the upper right corner and select Messaging.
  2. Tap To All or To Presenter.

    If you do not see the options To All or To Presenter, the presenter has disabled the option to send a group message. Skip to Step 3.

  3. Tap Enter Message field.

    The On-screen Keyboard is displayed.

  4. Enter a question or comment for the presenter or group.
  5. Tap Send.

     

    The sent message is displayed in blue.

  6. Tap to send the question or comment to the presenter only.

    Alternatively, tap to send the question or comment to the group, including the presenter.

    The sent message is displayed in blue.

  7. Tap in the upper right corner and select Presentation to return to the current question.

    To close the keyboard, tap anywhere in the empty content view.

    To minimize the keyboard, tap the back button.
