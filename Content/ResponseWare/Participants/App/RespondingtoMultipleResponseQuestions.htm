Responding to Multiple Response Questions

Multiple Response questions allow participants to send more than one response to a question.

  1. When polling is open, the question and answer choices are displayed.
  2. Tap the number(s) or letter(s) that correspond to the answer choices.

    The presenter may have enabled to allow duplicate answers, which you are permitted to select the same answer choice more than once

    Answer selections can be changed while polling is open.

    After polling is closed, the results are displayed.

  3. To view the question, tap in the upper right of the screen.
  4. To return to the chart, tap in the upper right of the screen.
