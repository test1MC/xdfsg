Technical Requirements

The following technical requirements are required for ResponseWare to properly function:

  • iPhone®, iPod Touch®, iPad® with iOS 7.0 or higher
  • Android smartphones or tablets with OS 4.1 or higher

    Android users with firmware versions 4.0 - 4.0.4 must access ResponseWare via the Chrome browser. See the ResponseWare Web for Participants User Guide.

  • Wi-Fi connection (recommended for optimal performance) or data connection
  • TurningPoint 5 or higher
  • TurningPoint Cloud

You may be charged by your wireless provider if you do not have an unlimited data plan. Turning Technologies is NOT responsible for any cellular usage charges.
