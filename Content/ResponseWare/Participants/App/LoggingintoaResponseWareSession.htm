Logging into a ResponseWare Session

Most presenters require participants to be signed into a Turning Account. Alternatively, presenters may allow participants to sign in as guests.

Participants can only join one session at a time.

  1. Open ResponseWare on your iOSAndroid device.

    If a Turning Account is not required to submit responses, skip to Step 4.

  2. Tap and select Sign In.

    To prevent your phone or tablet from locking during a presentation, tap , select Settings and tap the check boxslider button labeled Keep Device AwakePrevent Auto-Lock.

  3. Enter the Email and Password associated with your Turning Account and tap Sign In.

    To change your password or create a new account, tap Forgot your password? or Create an account.

  4. Enter the Session ID provided by the presenter in the Enter Session ID field.

  5. Tap Join Session.
  6. If prompted, enter additional information, such as first and last name, email address, or User ID. Required information must be entered.

    User ID may refer to a Student ID, section number or any other identification required by the presenter.

  7. Tap Submit.

    The first question appears on-screen after the presenter opens polling.

  8. Tap the button that corresponds to your answer choice.

    The results appear after polling is closed.
