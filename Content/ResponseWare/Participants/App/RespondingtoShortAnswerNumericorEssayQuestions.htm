Responding to Short Answer, Numeric or Essay Questions

  1. When polling is open, the question is displayed.

  2. Tap the blankEnter Answer field next to Send.

    The On-screen Keyboard is displayed.

  3. Enter a response by typing your answer.

    Numeric Response questions only accept numbers, one decimal point, and +/- value at the beginning of an answer.

  4. Tap Send.

    If your answer was successfully sent, the message Response Received is displayed.

    Answers may be edited while polling is open and the question is not set to only accept your first response.

    When polling is closed, your answer is displayed.
