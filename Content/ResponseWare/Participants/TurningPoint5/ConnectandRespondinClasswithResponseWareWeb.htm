Connecting and Responding in Class with ResponseWare Web

  1. Go to https://responseware.turningtechnologies.com.

  2. Enter the Session ID provided by your instructor in the Session ID field.
  3. Click Join Session.
  4. If prompted, sign in to your Turning Account by entering your email and password and click Sign In.
  5. If prompted, enter additional information, such as first and last name, email address or User ID. Required information must be entered.

    User ID can refer to a Student ID, section number or any other identification required by the presenter.

  6. Click Join.

    The first question appears on-screen after the presenter opens polling. Click the button that corresponds to your answer choice.

    The results appear after polling is closed.
