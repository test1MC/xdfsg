Register Your Device ID

You may be required to register your ResponseWare ID to receive credit in class. When using ResponseWare, your ResponseWare ID is your Device ID. Depending on your instructor, you may need to register your Device ID through your school's LMS (Blackboard, Moodle, WebCT, Sakai, etc.) or through the Turning Technologies Web Registration Tool.

If your organization uses an LMS integration, you must register your Device ID through this integration to receive credit. You cannot register your ResponseWare ID through your Turning Account.
