Connecting and Responding in Class with the ResponseWare App

There is a free ResponseWare app for Android and iOS web-enabled devices, such as smartphones and tablets.

 

Download the free ResponseWare app for your web-enabled device.

  • Android devices - Download from the Google Play Store
  • iOS (Apple) devices - Download from the App Store or iTunes

If downloading to an iPad, tap the iPhone option to access the application.

  1. Open the ResponseWare app on your Android or iOS device.
  2. Enter the Session ID provided by your instructor.
  3. Tap Join Session.
  4. If prompted, sign in to your Turning Account by entering your email and password and tap Sign In.
  5. If prompted, enter additional information, such as first and last name, email address, or User ID.

    Required information must be entered.

    User ID can refer to a Student ID, section number or any other identification required by the presenter.

  6. Tap Submit.

    The first question appears on-screen after the presenter opens polling. Tap the button that corresponds to your answer choice.

    The results appear after polling is closed.
