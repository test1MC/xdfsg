Responding to Multiple Response Questions

Multiple Response questions allow participants to send more than one response to a question.

  1. When polling is open, the question and answer choices are displayed.

  2. Click the number(s) or letter(s) that correspond to the answer choices.

    The presenter may have enabled the option to allow duplicate answers, which allows you to select the same answer choice more than once.

    Answer selections can be changed as long as polling is open. Click Clear to remove answer selections.

  3. After polling is closed, the results are displayed.

  4. To view the question, click Question from the left menu .
  5. To return to the chart, click Chart from the left menu .
