Messaging the Presenter and the Group

Participants can send questions or comments to the presenter via the Messaging tab. The presenter may enable an option that allows participants to message the entire group, including the presenter.

  1. From the left menu, click Messaging.
  2. Click To All or To Presenter.
  3. Click the blank field next to Send.

  4. Enter a question or comment for the presenter or group.
  5. Click Send.

    The message is displayed.

  6. Click Question from the left menu to return to the current question.
