Logging into a ResponseWare Session

Follow the steps below to sign in to your Turning Account, join a session and answer polling questions.

If a Turning Account is not required to submit responses, skip to step 4.

  1. Go to https://responseware.turningtechnologies.com.
  2. Click Sign In.
  3. Enter your Email, Password and click Sign In.

    To create a new account, click Create an account.

  4. Enter the Session ID in the box provided.

    The Session ID is provided in class or presentation by the presenter.

  5. Click Join Session.
  6. If prompted, enter additional information such as first and last name, email address or User ID. Required information must be entered.

    A User ID may be a Student ID, section number or any other identification required by the presenter.

  7. Click Join.

    The first question appears on-screen after the presenter opens polling.

  8. Click the button that corresponds to your answer choice.

    The results appear after polling is closed.
