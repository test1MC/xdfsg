Transitioning from a ResponseWare Account to a Turning Account

ResponseWare 2.0 requires a Turning Account. A previous ResponseWare account and any applicable license will be transferred to the Turning Account. See the steps below if an email was not received to migrate from the ResponseWare account to the Turning Account.

  1. Go to https://account.turningtechnologies.com.
  2. Click Forgot your password?
  3. Enter your email address in the field provided.

    You must enter the same email address you used for your previous ResponseWare account.

  4. Click Reset Password.

    A link is emailed to you to reset your password.

 

When you receive the email to reset your password, click on the link provided and follow the steps. Once the password is reset, you will be able to sign in to ResponseWare.
