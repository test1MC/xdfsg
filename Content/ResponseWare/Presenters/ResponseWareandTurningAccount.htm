ResponseWare and Turning Account

A Turning Account Username and password is required to sign in to TurningPoint CloudResponseWare in TurningPoint 5. A Turning Account can be created at no cost. The Turning Account secure sign in and file encryption ensures that data is safeguarded in TurningPoint Cloud.

If you do not have a Turning Account, see Creating a Turning Account. If you have a Turning Account, skip Creating a Turning Account.
