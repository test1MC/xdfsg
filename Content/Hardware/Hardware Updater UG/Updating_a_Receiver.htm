Updating a Receiver

Hardware Updater 2.0 supports RF (RRRF-02), RF+ (RRRF-03), RF (RRRF-04) and Proctor (RRRF-03-P) receivers.

  1. Close out of all other Turning Technologies applications and unplug all other Turning Technologies hardware.
  2. Open the Hardware Updater.
  3. Plug in the RRRF-02, RRRF-03, RRRF-04 or RRRF-03-P receiver.

    Only plug in one device at a time.

    The version number will turn black if the device does not have the latest firmware version.

  4. Click Update Firmware.

    DO NOT UNPLUG THE DEVICE DURING THE UPDATE!

  5. Unplug the device only after the prompt indicates that it is safe to do so.

    The device now has the latest firmware.
