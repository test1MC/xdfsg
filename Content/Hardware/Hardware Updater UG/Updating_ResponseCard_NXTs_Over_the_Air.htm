Updating ResponseCard NXTs Over the Air

The ‘over the air’ option is useful if a large quantity of ResponseCard NXTs need updated. The over the air process requires a RRRF-03, RRRF-04 or RRRF-03-P receiver and a minimum ResponseCard NXT firmware of 1.0.1.

Find the firmware version of a ResponseCard NXT by pressing the softkey below the Wrench icon. Press the right arrow until Device Info appears on-screen. Press the center Enter key, then press the right arrow.

  1. Open the Hardware Updater.
  2. Plug in a RRRF-03, RRRF-04 or RRRF-03-P receiver.
  3. Click the Update NXT Devices Over the Air tab.
  4. Verify the channel on the receiver matches the channel on the ResponseCards.

    A warning message will appear if another receiver within 200 feet is on the same channel.

  5. Click Start Update.
  6. Press any number button on the ResponseCard NXT to begin the download.

    ResponseCards updating to the new firmware appear under the Updating tab.

    ResponseCards that have completed updating to the new firmware appear under the Current tab.

    ResponseCards that did not successfully download the new firmware appear under the Needs Attention tab.
