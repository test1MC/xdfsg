Using the Over the Air Recovery Mode

Recovery mode is used to restore ResponseCard NXTs with black screens.

  1. Place a check in the box next to Recovery Mode.
  2. Click Start Update.
  3. Remove the batteries from the ResponseCard NXT.
  4. Press and hold the five (5) button while reinserting the batteries.
  5. Release the five (5) button after the batteries are securely reinserted.

    The ResponseCard NXT will restart updating in recovery mode.

  6. Repeat steps 3-5 for any additional ResponseCard NXTs.
  7. Click Stop after all ResponseCard NXTs have completed updating.
