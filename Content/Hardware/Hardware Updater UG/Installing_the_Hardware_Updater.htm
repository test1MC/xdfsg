Installing the Hardware Updater

Please be sure you have full administrative privileges to download and install software. If you are unsure, check with your IT Administrator.

  1. Double-click the saved setup file.
  2. Click Next on the InstallShield Wizard.
  3. Select I accept the terms in the license agreement or I do not accept the terms in the license agreement. Selecting ‘I do not accept the terms in the license agreement’ cancels the install.
  4. Click Next on the InstallShield Wizard.
  5. Optionally, enter your Username and Organization.
  6. Select to allow Anyone who uses this computer (all users) or Only for me access to the Hardware Updater.
  7. Click Next on the InstallShield Wizard.
  8. Click Install on the InstallShield Wizard to complete the installation.
  9. Click Finish to exit the InstallShield Wizard.
