Additional Solutions

Visit http://www.einstruction.com/products for information about AdjustIt3™ and the Touch Board Wireless Kit.

The AdjustIt3™ Stand provides a mobile solution to move from class to class and adjusts to any height to accommodate all users. Compatible with a wide range of short throw projectors, the mobile stand includes wheels with brakes for greater stability.

The Touch Board Wireless Kit offers maximum flexibility in the classroom. A wireless communication hub and receiver (RF Hub and RFCOM) allow for wireless connection to the computer so that the Touch Board can be placed anywhere in the room.
