Connecting the Video Projector

For optimal usage and viewing, the minimum required screen resolution should be set at 1024 x 768.

  1. With the computer turned on, connect the projector video cable from the computer to the video projector.
  2. Turn on the video projector.
  3. With the video projector aimed at the Touch Board Plus, align the projected image onto the Touch Board Plus so that it is within the board's active area.
