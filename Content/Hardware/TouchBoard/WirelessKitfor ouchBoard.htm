Wireless Kit for Touch Board Plus

Turning Technologies Device Manager version 7.3 or higher must be installed on your computer prior to installing the Wireless Kit.

Device Manager is automatically installed with the following software: WorkSpace (v.9.4) and Flow (v.2.0).

The MultiHub plugs into a USB port on your computer or a powered USB Hub. The RFCOM (Radio Frequency Communicator) plugs into a wall outlet while positioned in the RFCOM clip that slips over the frame at the side of the Touch Board.
