Setting Up the Pen Station

The Pen Station conveniently clips to the side of the DualBoard so that the pens are able to charge when not in use.

  1. Insert the clip into the Pen Station.
  2. Attach the Pen Station to the side of the DualBoard.

    Adjust the Pen Station by sliding the device to the desired location.

  3. Connect one end of the power cord to the Pen Station and connect the other end to the controller.
  4. Insert each pen tip-down into the Pen Station.
