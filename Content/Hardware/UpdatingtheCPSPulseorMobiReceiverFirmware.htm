Updating the CPS Pulse™ or Mobi™ Receiver Firmware

  1. Download Device Manager.
  2. Remove all of the receivers or other Turning Technologies hardware form the computer you are using.
  3. Install Device Manager.
  4. Restart your computer.
  5. Open Device Manager and plug in the receiver you are going to update.
  6. Select the appropriate receiver in Device Manager, click File and select Preferences.

  7. From the Preferences window click the Firmware Update tab and click Check for Updates.

  8. A message displays alerting you whether or not firmware updates are available. Click OK.

  9. Click Next on the confirmation screen. A list of firmware updates for your device is displayed.
  10. Select the boxes in the Device column that require updating and click Update.

  11. Click OK to continue with the update.

    When the update is complete the following screen will appear.

  12. Click Finish.

    Unplug the receiver from your computer as needed. If you have additional receivers to update for additional devices, repeat steps 5 - 12 with the next receiver.
