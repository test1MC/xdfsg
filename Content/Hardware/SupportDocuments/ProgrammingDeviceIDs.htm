Programming Device IDs

You should only program your ResponseCard to a different Device ID if requested to do so.

ResponseCard XR must be programmed with the ResponseCard Programmer. For more information, contact Turning Technologies toll-free at 866.746.3015 or email support@turningtechnologies.com.

ResponseCard RF-02 and RF-03

There is a maximum of five seconds to complete each step before the programming process times out.

  1. Press the Channel, CH or GO button.
    The LED will flash green and red.
  2. Press the ? button.
    The LED will flash green and red.
  3. Enter the new six-digit Device ID using keys ‘0’ to ‘9’ (valid Device IDs are 000001 to 999999).
    Entering ‘000000’ will reset the Device ID to factory default.
  4. Press the ? button.
    The LED will flash yellow.
  5. Press the Channel, CH or GO button.
    The LED will flash green to indicate a successful Unit ID change.

ResponseCard NXT

  1. Press the soft key under the Wrench icon.
  2. Use the right arrow to scroll to Device Info and press the Enter/center button.
  3. Press the Channel button, the Channel button, the ABC button and finally the Enter/center button.
  4. Enter the new six-digit Device ID using keys ‘0’ to ‘9’ (valid Device IDs are 000001 to 999999).Entering ‘000000’ will reset the Device ID to factory default.
  5. Press the soft key under OK.
