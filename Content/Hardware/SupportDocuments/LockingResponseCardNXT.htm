How to Lock the
ResponseCard NXT

Locking the ResponseCard NXT prevents participants from changing the channel on the ResponseCard. Follow the steps below to lock the ResponseCard NXT. To unlock the ResponseCard NXT, simply follow the same sequence.

All four steps must be completed within three (3) seconds.

  1. Press the Channel button
  2. Press the abc button.
  3. Press the abc button.
  4. Press the Channel button.
