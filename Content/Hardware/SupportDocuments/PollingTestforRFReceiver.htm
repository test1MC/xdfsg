Running a Polling Test

  1. Plug in the receiver.
  2. Open TurningPoint.
  3. Click the receiver channel.
  4. If the receiver channel does not match the channel number on the ResponseCards, click the channel number. Select the correct channel from the drop-down menu and click Test.

  5. Press 1/A on the ResponseCard. A response indicates that the receiver and ResponseCards are communicating properly.

    When a receiver is in use within 200 feet of another user, each receiver must be set to its own channel.
