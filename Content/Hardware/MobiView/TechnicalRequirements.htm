Technical Requirements

MobiView is designed to function with most computers; however, there are some basic hardware and software requirements.

PC

  • Microsoft Windows® XP SP2, Vista, 7 or 8
  • Intel® Pentium® dual-core processor, 2 GHz or higher (or equivalent)
  • 2 GB RAM
  • 700 MB hard disk space for installation (600 MB additional for Gallery content)
  • DVD-ROM drive for installation
  • XVGA (1024x768) or higher resolution with a minimum of 24-bit color
  • Video card with support for DirectX 9 or higher
  • Speakers and microphone for audioand video files
  • Adobe® Flash® Player 9.0 or higher
  • Standard USB 2.0 port (for USB-based hardware devices)

Mac

  • OS X v10.7, 10.8 or 10.9
  • PowerPC G4 1.66 GHz or higher processor
  • 2 GB RAM
  • 1.8 GB hard disk space for installation
  • 1024x768 or higher resolution
  • DVD-ROM drive for installation
  • Java® 6 or higher
  • “libUSB” (USB support)
  • Standard USB 2.0 port (for USB-based hardware devices)

Linux®

  • Ubuntu 12.04 LTS or 14.04
  • Intel® Pentium® IV Processor 1 GHz or higher (or equivalent)
  • 2 GB RAM
  • 1.5 GB hard disk space for installation
  • 1024x768 or higher video resolution
  • Linux with 2.6 kernel or higher
  • DVD-ROM drive for installation
  • “bluez-libs”-libbluetooth1 (Bluetooth support)
  • “libUSB” (USB support)
  • XDG compliant (menus and file association)
  • "bc" utility
  • "finger" utility
  • libstdc++6
  • "sudo" version 1.6.7p5 or higher
  • "ia32-libs" (64-bit support)
  • Standard USB 2.0 port (for USB-based hardware devices)
