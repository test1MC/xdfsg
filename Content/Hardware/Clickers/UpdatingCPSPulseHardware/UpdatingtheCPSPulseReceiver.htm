Updating the CPS Pulse Receiver

  1. Plug in the CPS Pulse RF hub into an available USB port on the computer.
  2. Open Device Manager.
  3. Click File and select Preferences.

  4. Click the Firmware Update tab.

  5. Click Check for Updates.

    The Firmware Update message appears.

  6. Click OK and then Next.

  7. Select the boxes in the Device column that require updating and click Update.
  8. Click OK to continue with the update.

    When the update is complete the following screen will appear.

  9. Click Finish.
