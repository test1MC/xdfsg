Updating the CPS Pulse SRS Devices

  1. From the CPS RF(Pulse) Properties window click the Update Keypads tab.

    You will be directed to the appropriate default folder: Program Files/eInstruction/Device manager/Firmware Updates/CPSPulse/Languages.

  2. Select the folder (typically there will only be one folder) that contains the appropriate firmware version and select the appropriate language.

  3. Click Update.
  4. An information box will appear, click OK to perform the update.

  5. A message will appear requesting you to have your devices join a specific channel. Enter the appropriate channel number on all devices that require the update. When all of your devices have joined, click OK.

  6. An information box will appear. Do not alter the clickers or Device Manager during the update process. Once the update is complete, the clickers will turn off automatically. Click OK to continue.

    The Sending Firmware Data window opens.

    The update may take a few moments. The down arrow on the devices should be blinking to indicate that data is being transferred.

    Two messages will appear on the devices.

    The first indicates the progress of the data transfer ( Receiving xxxx/xxxx ) and the second indicates the progress of the data verification ( Checking CRS xxxxx/xxxx)

  7. When the verification is complete the devices will turn off. When all devices have turned off, click OK on the Sending Firmware Data window to complete the update and return to Device Manager.
