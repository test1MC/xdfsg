Responding to Short Answer and Numeric Response Questions

  1. Press a button on the keypad to “wake up” the ResponseCard.
  2. To choose a character, press the left or right arrow buttons to scroll through the list. While the cursor is on the letter you would like to use, press the SEL button.

    Press the abc button to switch between alpha or numeric entry. Press the delete button if you make a mistake.

  3. Press the Enter button to send your response.

    A checkmark appears on the right side of the screen to confirm that your answer was received.
