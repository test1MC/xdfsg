About ResponseCard Programmer

The ResponseCard Programmer allows users to quickly and easily program ResponseCards to a new Unit ID. The ResponseCard Programmer supports three types of ResponseCards:

  • ResponseCard RF - RRRF-02 and RRRF-03
  • ResponseCard XR-01 - 1.2 firmware or higher
  • ResponseCard NXT - 0.7.5 firmware or higher

    The ResponseCard Programmer is NOT compatible with Response Card XR-02.
