Changing ResponseCard RF, XR-01 and NXT Channels

The ResponseCard Programmer and ResponseCards must be on the same channel in order to communicate.

How to change ResponseCard RF channels...

  1. Press the CH button. A red and green light will flash.
  2. Enter the two-digit channel number. An amber light will flash.
  3. Press and release the CH button. A green light will flash.

How to change ResponseCard XR-01 channels...

  1. Press the Menu button.
  2. Press the down arrow/Yes button until Change Channel is highlighted. Press Enter.
  3. Enter the two-digit channel number and press Enter.

How to change ResponseCard NXT channels...

  1. Press Channel.
  2. Enter the two-digit channel number in the channel box.
  3. Press the Enter button.
