Logging Into a Test on a QT Device

  1. Press a button on the QT Device to "wake up" the device.
  2. The Welcome Screen is displayed followed by the Battery Indicator Screen.
  3. You may be prompted for Your ID.
    1. Press the Backspace key to clear the information displayed.
    2. Use the keyboard and/or number pad to enter Your ID.
    3. Press the function key below OK.
  4. You may be prompted for Test Version.
    1. Locate the Test Version number on the paper test.
    2. Use the number pad to enter the Test Version.
    3. Press the function key below OK.

    The test name, version number (if applicable) and question count are displayed.

  5. If the information displayed is correct, press the function key below OK.

    If the information is incorrect, press the far left function key, verify that the QT Device is on the correct channel and begin the process from step 1.

    You are now in Test Mode.
