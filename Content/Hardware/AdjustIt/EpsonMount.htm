Epson Mount

Ultra Short Throw Alternative for AdjustIt 3 Arm

  1. Assemble the two plates into one unit, securing them with large bolts, spring washers and nuts.

  2. Place the plastic cover on the mount.
  3. Secure the mount with bolts, spring washers and nuts.
  4. Place both knobs in the center holes to prevent the mount from swinging.
  5. Place the mount on the board and secure with knobs and flat washers.
  6. Attach the projector according to the Epson installation guide.
