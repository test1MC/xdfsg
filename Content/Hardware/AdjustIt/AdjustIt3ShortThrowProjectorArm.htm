AdjustIt 3
Short Throw Projector Arm

The AdjustIt3™ Short Throw Projector Arm provides a mounting solution for a wide range of short throw projectors.

It is recommended that a minimum of two people assemble the projector arm.

  1. Place the bottom plate with nuts facing up on a level surface.
  2. Place one spring on each nut.
  3. Line up holes on the upper plate with springs on the bottom plate and secure using all three round handles with studs.
  4. Use M8x16 screws and M8 washers to attach the appropriate projector holders.
    • Four projector holders are required. The placement of the projector mounting holes determines which holders are needed.
  5. Insert the M12x45 screw into the upper plate slot. Secure with two large M12 washers on each side of the top plate, two M12 nuts and the small M12 washer.
  6. Use M4x20 screws and M4 washers to attach the projector holders to the projector.

    Depending on your projector, you may need to use additional hardware to secure the projector holders to the projector.

  7. Attach the large M12 washer to the washer with nut.
  8. Run the power and video cables (VGA, HDMI, etc.) through the arm. Start at the slot located on the short part of the arm and feed through the top slot.
  9. Attach the arm to the central structure.
  10. Use two T-shaped handles with studs to secure the arm.
  11. Insert washer with nut into long arm slot.
  12. To move the projector arm:
    1. Lower the board and remove T-shaped handles with studs.
    2. Carefully swing the projector to the side and secure with T-shaped small handles with studs.
