AdjustIt 3 Wall Mount

The AdjustIt3™ Wall Mount allows you to mount the DualBoard or Touch Board to a wall.

It is recommended that a skilled professional assess the strength of the wall and determine the appropriate hardware required to securely attach the AdjustIt 3 to the wall. A minimum of two people are required to safely lift and mount the board to the wall.

What's in the Box?

(1) Central Structure (Wall Stand)
(1) Board Shelf
(1) Handle

(2) Brakes for DualBoard

(2) Brakes for Touch Board
Not pictured (10) M8x16 Screws
Not pictured (10) M8 Washers
Not pictured (2) M6 Washers
Not pictured (1) Hex Wrench
  1. Remove the board shelf that is taped to the center vertical support from the central structure.
  2. Attach the central structure to the wall.
  3. Use four screws and four washers to attach the board shelf to the bottom of the central structure. DO NOT COMPLETELY TIGHTEN.
  4. Use two screws and two M8 washers to attach the handle to the central structure.
  5. Mount the appropriate board:

    DualBoard

    1. Place the DualBoard over the center lip.
    2. Use two screws and two M6 washers to attach the DualBoard brakes from the central structure to the top of the board.

    Touch Board

    1. Attach the Touch Board brakes to the board.

      For 78 in. boards, place the brakes facing up. For 88 in. boards, place the brakes facing down.

    2. Place the Touch Board on the board shelf.
    3. Attach the brakes to the central structure.
  6. Push the board shelf back to secure the board from kicking out.
  7. Tighten the board shelf screws.
  8. If necessary, use the handle to adjust the board height. Lift the handle up to raise the board. To lower the board, lift the handle and push down on the board shelf.
