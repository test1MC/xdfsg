Asking Tagged Questions

When the tagged question is visible on your classroom computer screen, the tagged question icon will be active.

  1. Tap the Question Zone icon.
  2. Tap Timer to set the timer. Tap Plus or Minus to set the timer with more or less time.
  3. Ask your students the question and include all options (if multiple choice). Request students to answer by pressing the corresponding key on their clickers.
  4. Tap Start.
  5. If all students answer the question before the timer is finished, tap Stop.
