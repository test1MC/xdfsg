Displaying the Student Response Grid On-screen

You can display a Student Response Grid on the classroom computer screen so students can determine whether Insight 360 has accepted the answers from their student devices. Each numbered block corresponds to an enrolled student.

  1. In the upper-right corner of the Question Zone, tap the Grid icon.

    The Student Response Grid appears at the top of the classroom computer desktop.

    • A white block indicates that a student is enrolled in class.
    • A gray block indicates that a student devices has joined the class session.
    • A blue block indicates that Insight 360 has recorded the student’s response from his/her device.
