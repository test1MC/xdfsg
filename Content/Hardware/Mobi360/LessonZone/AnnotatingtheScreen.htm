Annotating the Screen

You can use the Mobi 360 digital pen as a mouse pointer, an annotation tool, or to control the Mobi 360. The Lesson Zone contains the various pen annotation tools and options.

The pen works on the primary display. It cannot cross to a second screen on an extended desktop.

As an annotation tool, the digital pen can:

  • Draw or write free form text in various colors on the screen
  • Highlight objects in various colors on the screen
  • Place lines and geometric shapes on the screen
  • Erase annotations on the screen
