Saving Screenshots

To save screenshots with the Mobi 360, follow the steps below.

  1. From the Lesson Zone, tap the Screenshot icon.

    The number in Screenshots Display increases by one.

    The screenshots are saved in a folder on the classroom computer’s hard drive. Insight 360 will store a maximum of 10,000 screenshots per session. The total number of screenshots saved is limited by the size of your computer’s hard drive.
