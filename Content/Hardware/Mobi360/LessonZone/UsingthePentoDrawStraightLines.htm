Using the Pen to Draw Straight Lines

You can draw straight lines with the Mobi 360 digital pen in two ways:

  • With the pen or highlighter selected hold the top rocker button down before touching the pen tip to the Mobi 360 writing surface, drag the pen across the writing surface, then release the rocker button before lifting the pen from the writing surface.
  • With the line tool selected drag the digital pen across the Mobi 360 writing surface.
  1. Tap Lesson.
  2. Tap the Line Tool icon.
