Using the Curtain to Hide the Screen

Tapping Curtain in the Lesson Zone displays a large black rectangle over the classroom computer desktop. This effectively covers the screen to hide the items (such as a CueTag) behind the curtain. You can move or resize the curtain to partially reveal the screen.

The Pen can be in any tool mode (for example: Mouse Pointer, Pen or Highlighter) when moving or resizing the curtain.

  1. From the Lesson Zone, tap the Curtain icon.

    The curtain appears on the screen and a blue box surrounds the selected Curtain icon.

  2. To move the Curtain:
    1. With the curtain showing, tap and hold the digital pen onto the Mobi 360 writing area. The screen cursor changes to a hand.
    2. Move the pen as you want the curtain moved.
  3. To resize the Curtain:
    1. The curtain has four resizing “handles,” one on each of its four sides. With the digital pen hovering over the Mobi 360 writing area, move the Pen over a Curtain handle. The cursor changes into a two-way arrow.
    2. Press and drag the tip of the pen to the desired location.
    3. Repeat for other curtain sides if necessary.
  4. To remove the Curtain, tap the Curtain icon from the Lesson Zone.

    The Curtain disappears and the blue box is removed from the Curtain icon.
