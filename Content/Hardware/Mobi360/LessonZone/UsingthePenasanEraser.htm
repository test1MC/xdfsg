Using the Pen as an Eraser

In the Lesson Zone, tap Eraser to use the digital pen to delete annotations that you placed on the classroom computer screen using the Mobi 360 digital pen. The Eraser cannot delete content produced by another source such as a Microsoft Word document or Internet web page. To delete all annotations, tap Delete.

  1. Tap Lesson.
  2. Tap the Eraser icon.
  3. To change the eraser size:
    1. With the Eraser selected, tap Size.
    2. Tap the eraser size of your choice.
