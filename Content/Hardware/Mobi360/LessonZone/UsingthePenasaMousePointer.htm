Using the Pen as a Mouse Pointer

Using the pen as a mouse pointer allows you to control the classroom computer desktop.

The pen works on the primary display. It cannot cross to a second screen on an extended desktop.

  1. Tap Lesson.
  2. Tap the Pointer icon.
    • To point, hold and move the digital pen just above the Mobi 360 writing surface.
    • To select an area, tap and drag the pen across the Mobi 360 writing surface.
    • To click, tap the writing surface once.
    • To right-click, press and hold the rocker button, tap once and release the rocker button.
