Using the Screenshot Sorter

The Sorter displays icons for five categories of screenshots:

User-triggered

ExamView Questions

Verbal Questions

Multi-user or Constructed Response mode

Tagged Questions

By default, screenshots from all categories display when the Sorter first opens. You can view screenshots only from a certain category by turning off the non-desired categories.

  1. In Lesson mode, tap the Screenshot Sorter icon.

    Thumbnails of screenshots display on the left side of the classroom computer screen.

  2. Tap the screenshot you want to display.

    If too many screenshots are displaying, tap the Sorter icons that are NOT related to the category of screenshot you are looking for, and then tap the screenshot you want to display.

    For example: If you are looking for a Verbal Question screenshot, tap the three icons OTHER than the Verbal Question icon.

  3. To close the screenshot, tap Desktop Display or Whiteboard Display.
