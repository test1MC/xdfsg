Displaying the Computer Screen from the Mobi 360

The Mobi 360 allows you to control your computer desktop from anywhere in the room.

If your computer is connected to a projector or large monitor, students can also view your computer desktop. This means that you can use Mobi 360 to present documents, Microsoft PowerPoint presentations, web pages, videos and other media from your computer.

  1. Tap Lesson.
  2. Tap Display.
  3. Tap Computer Desktop.

    You can use the other Mobi 360 digital pen tools to annotate the computer screen. Such annotations are simply overlaid on the screen and will not affect the underlying content.
