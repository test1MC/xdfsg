Adjusting the Brightness

Adjusting the amount of brightness will affect battery life; brighter screens take more battery. The Mobi 360 touch screen dims after a set amount of time to maximize battery life. You can adjust both the default screen brightness and the amount of time the screen can sit without being used before it dims.

  1. From the Mobi 360 Home screen, tap Settings.
  2. Use the digital pen to tap and hold the vertical line in the Brightness slider.
  3. With the pen tip on the vertical line, slide the vertical line:
    • Slide to the right to brighten the screen.
    • Slide to the left to darken the screen.
  4. Remove the pen tip from the slider.
  5. Optionally, in the Dim screen after section:
    • Tap Plus to add more time before the screen dims.
    • Tap Minus to subtract time before the screen dims.

    If the screen dims while you are teaching, tap the screen with the digital pen or your fingertip.
