About Mobi 360™

Mobi 360 is the mobile interactive whiteboard used to display coursework, annotate your computer desktop, and pose interactive questions.

The Mobi 360 comes with a receiver, USB cable, charging stand and digital pen.

The digital pen does not contain any ink and cannot be used to write on paper or surfaces other than the Mobi 360.

Through the Mobi 360, you can:

  • Navigate and control the classroom computer desktop
  • Access Favorites
  • Ask Verbal and Tagged questions
  • Interact with any software installed on your classroom computer by using the Mobi 360 as your keyboard and mouse
