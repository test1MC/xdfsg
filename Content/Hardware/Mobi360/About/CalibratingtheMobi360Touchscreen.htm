Calibrating the Mobi 360 Touchscreen

The touchscreen occasionally requires calibration. Recalibrating ensures that the Mobi 360 recognizes digital pen taps in the same place that you are actually tapping, writing or drawing on the touchscreen.

  1. From the Mobi 360 Home screen, tap Settings.

  2. Tap Calibrate Touch Screen.
  3. Follow the on-screen directions to tap each dot as it appears.

    Use the digital pen for better accuracy during the calibration process.

  4. Tap OK.
