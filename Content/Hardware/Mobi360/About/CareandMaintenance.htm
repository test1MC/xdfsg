Care and Maintenance

The Insight 360 system components are easy to maintain.

  • Store the Mobi 360, charging stand, digital pen, and student devices in a clean, dry place
  • Clean with a dry or lightly damp cloth

DO NOT SUBMERGE the Mobi 360, digital pen or student devices.

  • Periodically charge the battery on the Mobi 360 and its digital pen
  • Replace the batteries in the Mobi 360 and digital pen as needed
