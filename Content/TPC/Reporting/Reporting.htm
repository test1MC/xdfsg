Reports

There are eight types of reports available.

This chapter covers:

  • Generating Reports
  • Exporting a Report
  • Exporting Multiple Reports
  • Saving and Printing a PDF Report
  • Results by Question
  • Results by Participant
  • Results Detail
  • Attendance
  • Results by Demographic
  • Demographic Comparison
  • Comparative Results
  • Team Points
  • Session Log Report

Each report can be customized by selecting the data options on the right side of the reports window.

Report data can be easily copied and pasted into other formats by using the CtrlCmd + C, CtrlCmd + V shortcuts on your keyboard. To select all report data, use the CtrlCmd + A shortcut.
