Exporting Multiple Reports

Multiple reports can be simultaneously generated on the same session.

  1. While in the Reports window, click Export and select Excel.

    The Excel Workbook Export window opens.

  2. Select a report type from the drop-down menu in the upper right corner of the Reports window.

    The report is added to the Excel Workbook Export window.

    The Excel Workbook Export window will remain open behind the TurningPoint window as reports are added. If the Excel Workbook Export window is closed, click in the Reports window and select View Current Excel Workbook.

  3. Repeat step 2 as needed.
  4. Click Export Workbook from the Excel Workbook Export window to generate the workbook.

    The selected reports will be included as individual worksheet tabs.
