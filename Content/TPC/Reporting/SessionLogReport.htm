Session Log Report

The Session Log Report displays a log of session events and/or messages in chronological order.

  • Session Events- Displays slide show and question polling open and close time stamps.
  • Messaging- Displays all instances of messages sent during the session with a time stamp. Also includes information about who sent the message.
  • Participant Information- Includes participant ResponseWare information, session join/leave information and self-paced test information.
