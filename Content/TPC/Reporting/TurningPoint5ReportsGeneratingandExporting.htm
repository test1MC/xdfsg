Generating and Exporting TurningPoint Cloud Reports

There are nine types of session reports in TurningPoint Cloud:

  • Results by Question - The Results by Question report displays the question text, question type, answer choices, responses (percentage and count) and the chart. Additional display options include question statistics, difficulty and discrimination index, screenshots and overall standards.
  • Results by Participant - The Results by Participant report displays the participant information, question text and participant responses. Additional display options include correct answers, answer choices, response history, response times, overall standards and active participants only.
  • Results Detail - The Results Detail report displays the participant information, participant responses, participant points earned, score (overall percentage), participant list averages (points and percentage), and answer key information. Additional display options include specific participant information, active participants only, question results, expand the columns, total points and scores.
  • Attendance - The Attendance report displays the participant information and attendance status. An additional display option allows viewing of participants by present or absent only.
  • Results by Demographic - The Results by Demographic reports displays the question text, question type, answer choices, responses (percentage and count) and the chart. Additional display options include question statistics, screenshots, overall standards and demographics.
  • Demographic Comparison - The Demographic Comparison report allows you to compare multiple demographic filters on the same report for all questions in the session. View results by counts or percentages.
  • Comparative Results - The Comparative Results reports allows you to select two questions from the session and compare them side-by-side. The questions are selected from the panel on the right of the window. Check the Display Question boxes to display the text from each selection.
  • Team Points - The Team Points report is used for competitions and displays the point summary by question or team member.
  • Session Log Report - The Session Log Report displays a log of session events and/or messages in chronological order. Additional display options include session events, messaging and participant information.
