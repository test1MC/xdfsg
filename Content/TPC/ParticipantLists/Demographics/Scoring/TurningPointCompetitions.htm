TurningPoint Competitions and Scoring

Team scores are calculated by dividing the score displayed by the number of individuals on the team. Scores can be displayed in one of two ways:

  • Cumulative - SUM of all team members’ current scores divided by the number of individuals on the team
  • Single - ONLY the team scores of the previously polled question slide are displayed

The number of individuals figured into the team scores is dependent on whether or not the Include Non-Responders option is selected in the Software Preferences.

There are two ways for participants to join a team:

  • A manually created participant list
  • A Team Assignment slide
