Allowing Participants to Choose a Team

Participants may choose their own teams through the use of a Team Assignment slide. A participant list may still be used to track the results of individual participants.

  1. Select the Polling tab.
  2. If you are using a question list, select the question list under Content and click PowerPoint Polling. If you are not using a question list but rather a PowerPoint presentation, simply click PowerPoint Polling.

    If you are using a participant list with your question list/presentation, select the participant list from the Dashboard prior to opening PowerPoint Polling.

  3. Choose the place in your question list/presentation where
    you would like to insert the Team Assignment slide. Click
    the Competition icon and select Team Assignment.
  4. Enter a title and up to 10 teams in the fields provided.
  5. Click the Reset icon and select session.
  6. Run the presentation.

    Participants are assigned to the team they selected and
    associated to that team in leader boards and reports.
