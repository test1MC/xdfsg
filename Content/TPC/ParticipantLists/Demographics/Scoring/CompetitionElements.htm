Competition Elements

Click the Competition icon on the TurningPoint toolbar to insert any of the following slides.

Point values or correct answers need to be assigned to answer choices for participants or teams to accumulate points for competition.

Participant Leader Board

This slide displays the top individual participant(s) ranked by their total points.

Use the Slide Preferences Pane to adjust the type of participant information to be displayed, the number of participants to be shown on the leader board and the type of score calculation (cumulative or single).

Team Leader Board

The top teams ranked by their total points are displayed on this slide.

Use the Slide Preferences Pane to adjust the number of teams to be shown on the leader board and select the type of score calculation (cumulative or single). Selecting the option Include Teams with Points <1 displays teams with zero or negative points.

Team MVP

The Team MVP slide displays the individual participant on each with the most points.

Use the Slide Preferences Pane to adjust the type of participant information to be displayed, the number of teams to be shown on the leader board and the type of score calculation (cumulative or single). Selecting the option Include Teams with Points <1 displays teams with zero or negative points.

Fastest Responders

This slide displays the individual participant(s) who responded correctly and fastest to the most recent question. This slide requires a correct response on the preceding question.

Use the Slide Preferences Pane to display participants who responded correctly to the previous question, adjust the type of participant information to be displayed and the number of participants to be shown on the leader board.

Racing Leader Board

An animated, visual representation of the team scores are represented with this slide.

Use the Slide Preferences Pane to adjust the type of participant information to be displayed and the type of score calculation (cumulative or single). Also, select an animated image to represent all teams as well as an animation speed and direction.

Wager

The wager slide allows participants to wager a percentage of their points on the next question slide. If the participants fail to correctly answer the slide, the amount of points wagered is lost. If the participants successfully answer the question, they are awarded that percentage of points. If there is a correct answer value for the question, participants are also awarded those points in addition to the points wagered.

Use the Slide Preferences Pane to allow for automatic polling and different bullet choices.
