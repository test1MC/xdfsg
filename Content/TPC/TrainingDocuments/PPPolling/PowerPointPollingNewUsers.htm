Before downloading and using TurningPoint Cloud, you must have a Turning Account.

Getting Started

Creating a Turning Account and Downloading the Software

  1. Go to https://account.turningtechnologies.com/account/.
  2. Click Create Account.
  3. Enter your email address and click Next.
  4. Check your email and click the link to verify your Turning Account.
  5. After your Turning Account has been created, sign in to your Turning Account.
  6. Click Downloads from the left menu.
  7. Select TurningPoint Cloud.

    For more information on creating a Turning Account, downloading the TurningPoint Cloud software and creating participant lists, please refer to the Turning Account for Presenters document.
