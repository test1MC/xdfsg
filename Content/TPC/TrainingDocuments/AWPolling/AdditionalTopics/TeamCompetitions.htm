Team Competitions

Add competitions to polling to keep track of teams or participants with the most points and fastest responses. Several types of on-the-fly competitions are available.

  1. Click the Quick Poll Menu .
  2. Select Demographic Assignment (competition).

    Participants “click in” to choose a team.

  3. Close polling to display the percentage chosen for each team.

    Correct answers need to be assigned on competition questions for teams to accumulate points. To assign correct answers on-the-fly, right-click on the bar of the correct answer choice.
