Email Template

Below you will find an email template that can be used to notify your students about the use of clickers in your classroom. This template will require one (1) small modification to meet your specific needs. You will need to edit the following area, if not already edited by your Account Executive: School Code.

Send this email one to two (1 - 2) weeks prior to the start of classes.

 

Hello Students,

We will be using Turning Technologies clickers in our class this semester. Please purchase the following prior to the first day of class:

  • Turning Account license

Visit the campus bookstore or store.turningtechnologies.com to purchase the items listed above.

Create a Turning Account and enroll in the course at account.turningtechnologies.com.

Log in to your LMS (Blackboard, Canvas, D2L, Moodle, Sakai) and follow the links for Turning Technologies to create a Turning Account, purchase the clicker and license (if you have not already done so), and enroll in the course.

The School Code for store.turningtechnologies.com is: _____________

Please purchase the and Turning Account license, as well as create a Turning Account, by the first Friday of class.

I hope you have found this information helpful. If you have any questions, please let me know.
