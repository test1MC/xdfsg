TurningPoint Cloud Implementation Agenda

Listed below are items to assist in the transition to TurningPoint Cloud.

Software

  • TurningPoint Cloud must be downloaded from https://account.turningtechnologies.com. A Turning Account must be created prior to signing in to the Turning Account website.

Turning Account Creation

  • Participants and Presenters (Instructors) must create individual Turning Accounts. Presenters need a Turning Account to open the software and to access data and participant lists within TurningPoint Cloud.
  • Participants are required to "register" their device (ResponseCard and/or ResponseWare) via the Turning Account Registration website. Participant data will not be uploaded into the appropriate Learning Management System (LMS) if the participant does not create a Turning Account.
  • Licensing definitions:
    • Turning Account - A unique identifier that is used to tie together all software accounts and response devices. Turning Accounts are required for use with Turning Point Cloud and ResponseWare.
    • Turning Account License - A license required for responses from response devices or ResponseWare to be tracked and used in TurningPoint Cloud and Learning Management Systems (LMS).
    • ResponseWare Presentation License - A license that enables participants using the ResponseWare app to respond to polling questions.
    • ResponseWare Concurrent License - A license that enables presenters to allow participants who do not have Turning Accounts to respond to polling questions.

Resident Computers at Podiums/Laptop Computers

  • Software must be updated on each individual computer. At this time an MSI is unavailable, however an image can be installed and mass deployed.
  • After download/installation of software, uninstall existing version of TurningPoint with TurningPoint Cloud.

Storage Receivers

  • TurningPoint Cloud must be downloaded from https://account.turningtechnologies.com.
  • Save file to flash drive (storage receiver). Download and open software. If necessary, create a Turning Account from the Turning Account Registration link.

Learning Management System (LMS)

  • Blackboard, Moodle and Sakai all require updated building blocks.

    All registrations made in the old building blocks MUST be re-registered.

  • Canvas and Desire2Learn both require new LTI links.
  • The LMS campus contact must be in connection (ASAP) with the Turning Technologies Strategic Integrations team (Alan Johnson or T.C. Osman).
  • Participants must register their ResponseCard or ResponseWare IDs via the LMS. A new registration link will be made available by LMS administrators. Participants will be directed to the Turning Account website where they will register their Device ID.

Purchasing ResponseCards/ResponseWare/Turning Account License

  • Participants can visit store.turningtechnologies.com to purchase ResponseCards, ResponseWare and/or Turning Account license options. Alternatively, participants can access the Student Store via the License tab within their Turning Account. Within the Student Store, different purchasing options are available:
    • ResponseCard bundled with one year use of ResponseWare PLUS a one year Turning Account
    • ResponseWare only PLUS Turning Account license
    • Turning Account license bundled with one year of ResponseWare (for existing ResponseCards)
    • Used ResponseCard - Price of device PLUS purchase Turning Account license bundled with one year of ResponseWare

Turning Technologies Website/Training Documents/User Guides

  • Training documents and user guides will be available to support both presenters and participants in the implementation of TurningPoint Cloud. Visit the Training Home Page and User Guides Home Page.
  • Additionally, online training and tutorials will be available at the Training Home Page.
  • Presenters can attend one of the Getting Started webinars hosted by the Implementation Specialists team.
  • Ask your Implementation Specialist for web page documentation and additional support.
