Converting TurningPoint 2008 Presentations

Presentations created in TurningPoint 2008 can be converted to TurningPoint and used for polling.

  2. Click PowerPoint Polling.

    PowerPoint opens with the TurningPoint Cloud ribbon.

  3. Open a TurningPoint 2008 presentation.

    A warning message appears.

  4. Click OK to continue the conversion.

    A second warning message appears.

    Converting Graph charts from presentations created in TurningPoint 5 or TurningPoint Cloud prior to 6.2 is optional. For more information, see Converting Graph Charts to Excel Charts on page 1.

  5. Click Yes to convert the Graph charts to Excel charts. Click No to keep the Graph charts.

    The presentation will continue to work with Graph charts.

    The TurningPoint 2008 presentation is converted to the new format.

  6. Save the presentation.

    Save your PowerPoint presentation in the TurningPoint Cloud Content folder. The presentation is listed in the Content section of the Polling tab for quick access.
