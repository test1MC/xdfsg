Locking the Receiver Channel

Administrators have the ability to lock the channel to prevent users from changing channels in shared environments.

  1. Open TurningPoint Cloud as the Administrator.
  2. Sign in to your Turning Account.
  3. Click in the bottom right corner.

  4. Select Connections from the left panel.
  5. Below the Response Devices section, select the desired channel and, optionally, select Force Channel Change. The Force Channel Change option, if selected, allows the receiver channel to be maintained once selected.
  6. Click Lock Receiver Channels.
  7. Click Close.

    To remove the channel lock, run TurningPoint Cloud as the Administrator and click Unlock Receiver Channels.
