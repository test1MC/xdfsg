Deleting Content

To delete content from TurningPoint Cloud, follow the steps below.

  3. Select a content item.
  4. Click the Content drop-down menu and select Delete.

    A confirmation window is displayed.

  5. Click Delete.

    Alternatively, select a content item and press the Delete key on the keyboard or right-clickcontrol-click to delete.

    Deleting a question list from TurningPoint Cloud also deletes the list from its default location and moves it to the Recycle BinTrash.
