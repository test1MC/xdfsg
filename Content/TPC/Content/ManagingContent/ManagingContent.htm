Managing Content

The TurningPoint Cloud Content tab allows a user to organize content into folders for all three polling environments. Content may be in the form of question lists or PowerPoint presentations.

This section covers the following topics:

  • Importing Content
  • Exporting Content
  • Converting an ExamView Test or CPS Export File into a Question List
  • Deleting Content
  • Refreshing Folder Content
