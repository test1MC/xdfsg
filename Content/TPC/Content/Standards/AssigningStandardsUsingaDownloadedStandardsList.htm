Assigning Standards Using a Downloaded Standards List

Once standards are downloaded, they can be assigned to questions in a question list. Standards may also be added to a session in the session editor. For more information, see Editing A TurningPoint Session on page 1.

 

At least one standard group must be downloaded.

  1. Click Standards in the bottom left of the Question List Editor screen.

    The Standards screen is displayed.

  2. Select a State or Country from the State or Country drop-down menu.
  3. Select a Topic from the Topic drop-down menu.
  4. Select a Subject from the Subject drop-down menu.
  5. Select a Grade from the Grade drop-down menu.

    All of the standards for the selected state/country, topic, subject and grade are displayed.

    TurningPoint Cloud will remember these selections for future use.

  6. Select a question from the left panel.

  7. Select a standard from the list. Click the right arrow icon to apply the standard to the question.
  8. Repeat steps 6 - 7 to assign standards to remaining questions.
  9. To remove a standard from a question, select the question and double-click the assigned standard.

    Optionally, click Clear All to remove all standards associated with the question.

  10. When finished, click Close.
