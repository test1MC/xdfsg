Updating Participant Lists from an LMS or the Turning Account Registration Website

TurningPoint Cloud can update participant list information from an LMS or the Turning Account Registration website.

An LMS integration must be configured on an LMS server. Confirm with your IT department that the integration is properly setup before attempting to use the TurningPoint Cloud integration.
