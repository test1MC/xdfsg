Deleting a Removed Participant from Results Manager

A participant can be permanently deleted after it is removed from Results Manager.

  3. Check Show Removed Participants at the bottom of the screen.

  4. Select the appropriate participant under the Removed Participants section.
  5. Click Delete Participant in the right panel.

    The Device ID and associated scores will remain stored under Unassigned Devices after the removed participant is permanently deleted from Results Manager.
