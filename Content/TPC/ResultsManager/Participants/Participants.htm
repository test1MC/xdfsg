Participants

Minor adjustments can be made to participant list data from Results Manager. For full participant list management information, see Managing Participant Lists on page 1. Adjustments to participant data will apply to all associated session files. If there are a large number of sessions affected, closing the Results Manager screen may take slightly longer than normal.
