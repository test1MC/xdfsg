Removing a Participant from the Participant List via Results Manager

Participants can be removed from within the Results Manager overview. Removing a participant from Results Manager will not remove the participant from session reports. However, they will not generate into participant reports or upload to an integration from Results Manager.

  3. Select a participant row.
  4. Click Remove Participant from the right panel.

 

To view a removed participant, see Viewing Removed Participants on page 1. To reinstate a participant, see Reinstating a Participant on page 1.
