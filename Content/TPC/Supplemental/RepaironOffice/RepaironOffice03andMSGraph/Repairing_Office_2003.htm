Repairing Office 2003

  1. Click the Windows Start icon.
  2. Select Control Panel.
  4. After all programs populate, select Microsoft Office 2003.
  5. Windows XP users, click Change, select Reinstall or Repair and click Next.
    1. Select Detect and Repair any errors in my Office installation.
    2. Click Install. Windows Vista and 7 users, click Repair.

  6. After the repair is complete, restart your computer.
