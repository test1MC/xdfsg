Enabling the Turning Point Add-in

Follow the steps below if the TurningPoint add-in becomes disabled.

How to enable the TurningPoint add-in for Office 2013 and 2010...

  1. Open PowerPoint.
  2. (Office 2013 only) Open a blank presentation.
  3. Click File and select Options.

    The PowerPoint Options window opens.

  4. Select Add-ins from the left menu.
  5. Select Disabled Items from the Manage drop-down menu and click Go.

    The Disabled Items window opens.

  6. Select TurningPoint and click Enable.
  7. Close PowerPoint.
  8. Open TurningPoint.

How to enable the TurningPoint add-in for Office 2007...

  1. Open PowerPoint.
  2. Click and select PowerPoint Options.

    The PowerPoint Options window opens.

  3. Select Add-ins from the left menu.
  4. Select Disabled Items from the Manage drop-down menu and click Go.

    The Disabled Items window opens.

  5. Select TurningPoint and click Enable.
  6. Close PowerPoint.
  7. Open TurningPoint.
