Taking Attendance in Anywhere Polling

  1. Open Anywhere Polling.
  2. From the Anywhere Polling showbar, click the Quick Poll Menu and select Attendance.

    The Attendance feature is only available when polling is closed.

    The Attendance Poll window appears.

  3. Instruct the students to press any number on their clicker or ResponseWare device.

    Device IDs are displayed if not listed in the participant list or if a participant list is not loaded.

  4. Click Present to display the names or Device IDs of participants in attendance. Alternatively, click Absent to display the names of participants who are not in attendance.
  5. When you are finished taking attendance, click Close Polling.

Attendance can be polled multiple times throughout the session.
