Using Keynote with Anywhere Polling or TurningPoint Anywhere

Open Keynote .

From the Menu Extras area, click Keynote and select Preferences.

Select Slideshow .

Check Allow Exposé, Dashboard and others to use screen.

Launch the Anywhere Polling or TurningPoint AnyWhere showbar.

From the Menu Extras area, click Window and select Bring All to Front.

The showbar now floats on top of Keynote.
