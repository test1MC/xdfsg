Downloading Standards

Downloadable standards are available for the United States, Australia, Canada, Ireland and the United Kingdom. Common Core Standards are also available. Downloaded standards are saved to the local computer and cannot be saved to a flash drive.

An active internet connection is required to download standards.

 

  1. Click Standards on the bottom left of the Question List Editor screen.

    The Standards screen is displayed.

  2. Click the Add/Remove/Update Standards icon.

    The Standards Selection window is opened.

  3. Check the standard or standards to be downloaded.

    To remove standards, uncheck the box or boxes.

  4. Click Save.

    The selected standards are downloaded or removed. Depending on the internet connection, this may take a few moments. Once finished, a confirmation box appears.

  5. Click OK.

 

To associate downloaded standards with questions, follow the instructions in the next section, Assigning Standards Using a Downloaded Standards List.
