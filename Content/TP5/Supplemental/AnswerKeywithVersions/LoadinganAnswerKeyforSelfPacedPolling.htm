Loading an Answer Key for Self-Paced Polling

Whether an answer key has multiple versions, the steps for polling are the same. The difference between a single version answer key and a multiple version answer is that the participants will need to enter version number in the response device.

  1. Open TurningPoint.
  2. Select your participant list and answer key from the Content section of the Dashboard.
  3. Select Self-Paced Polling.
  4. Click Accept Logins and make any necessary selections.

    The participants login with their response device and if there are multiple versions of the answer key they will be required to enter their version code.

  5. Click Start Polling to begin the test. After the participants take the test, click Stop Polling and save the session.
