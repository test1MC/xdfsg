Inserting Stats

Stats objects reveal statistics about responses after polling is closed. The value of the statistic is displayed in the stats object during a presentation after polling has been closed.

  1. Select the polling slide.
  2. From the TurningPoint ribbon/toolbar click the Objects icon, mouse over Stats and select the desired stat.

    Office 2007/2010/2013

    Office 2003

    Office 2011

    There are four stats available.

  3. Select a different stat from the Objects menu to change the current stat on the slide.

    Multiple stats may be placed on a single slide.

  4. To remove a stat, simply delete the object from the slide.

    Stats can be formatted like regular PowerPoint objects. The prompt text can be adjusted in the preferences.
