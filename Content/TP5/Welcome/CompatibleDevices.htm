Compatible Devices

The following devices are compatible with TurningPoint:

  • ResponseCard IR, RF and RF LCD (PowerPoint Polling and Anywhere Polling)
  • ResponseCard XR and NXT (PowerPoint Polling, Anywhere Polling and Self-Paced Polling)
  • QT Device (PowerPoint Polling, Anywhere Polling and Self-Paced Polling)
  • PresenterCard (PowerPoint Polling and Anywhere Polling)
  • ResponseWare (PowerPoint Polling and Anywhere Polling)
  • PresenterWare (PowerPoint Polling and Anywhere Polling)
