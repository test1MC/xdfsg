Fastest Responders

The Fastest Responders slide displays the participant(s) that responded the fastest and correctly to the question asked.

  1. Close polling to display the chart window.
  2. Expand the bottom of the window and click the Competition icon.
  3. Select Fastest Responders.

    The times will display next to the participants' names or Device IDs.

  4. Click the Competition icon again and select None to return to the chart.
