Wager Slide

The wager slide allows participants to wager a percentage of their points on the next question slide. If the participant fails to correctly answer the slide, the amount of points wagered is lost. If the participant successfully answers the question, they are awarded that percentage of points. If there is a correct answer value for the question, participants are also awarded those points in addition to the points waged.

  1. From the TurningPoint ribbon/toolbar, click the Compete icon and select Wager.
