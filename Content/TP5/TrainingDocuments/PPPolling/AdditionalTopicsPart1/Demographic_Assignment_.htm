Demographic Assignment

Inserting a Demographic Assignment Slide

The Demographic Assignment slide assigns participants to demographic groups. Assigning participants to a demographic allows you to view how each demographic answered during the presentation.

  1. From the TurningPoint ribbon/toolbar, click the New icon and select Demographic Assignment.

  2. Type the question and up to 10 possible answer choices.
  3. Add a demographic and adjust the slide preferences as needed.
