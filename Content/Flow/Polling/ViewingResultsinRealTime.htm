Viewing Results in Real-Time

Instructors can view incoming responses in real-time.

  1. Open Flow.
  2. Poll a question.
  3. While polling is opened, click Question statistics.

    The Question statistics window opens.

  4. Select the type of chart to display from the top left corner of the window.

Chart types vary depending on the question. Additionally, the last chart selected will be the first chart displayed with the next question.

For more information on question statistics and results,see Chart Options on page 1.
