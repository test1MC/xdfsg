Polling Impromptu Questions

This option is ideal if questions are based off of an image, webpage, PDF, etc. Impromptu questions are administered directly from the Main Window, allowing you to poll atop any application.

  1. Open Flow.
  2. Register participants. For more information, refer to Participants on page 1. For more information on registering participants, refer to the Participants chapter of the full Flow for PC, Mac and Linux user guide.
  3. Click Engage question.
  4. Select the question type from the Flow Main Window.

  5. Optionally, enter the question parameters.

  6. Click to begin the question .

  7. Click after all participants have answered.

    The Question statistics chart is displayed.

  8. Repeat steps 3 - 6 as necessary.

    Prior to ending the question, individual question progress can be monitored by clicking Question statistics.
