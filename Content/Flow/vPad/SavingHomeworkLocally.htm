Saving Homework Locally

If a participant elects to leave the Homework session, a message appears prompting you to Save locally, Discard or Return. If Save locally is selected, you can save the homework and upload the assignment at a later time.

The Save locally feature is only available for PC and Mac, and iPad and Android tablets.

  1. Leave the session.

    A message appears prompting you to Save locally, Discard or Return.

  2. Tap Save locally.

    The homework is saved to your device.
