Logging into a vPad Session

  1. Open vPad on the device or computer.
  2. Tap/click the Enter Your Student ID field and enter your Student ID.
  3. Tap/click the Enter vPad Session ID field and enter the Session ID.

    Optionally, select the appropriate session from the list of available sessions. UPnP protocol must be enabled on the local network.

  4. Tap/click .

    You have now joined the session.
