Changing the Receiver Channel

The receiver channel can be changed via Device Manager.

Flow supports the use of both infrared (IR) and radio frequency (RF) technology. Infrared devices utilize line of sight technology and therefore do not require channels. See Compatible Devices on page 1 for a list of supported devices.

Close Flow before opening Device Manager.

  1. Plug the receiver into an available USB port.
  2. Click in the notification are (PC)/Menu Extras area (Mac)/Dash Home (Linux) and select eInstruction Device Manager.
  3. Select the receiver image.
  4. Click .
  5. From the Base Channel menu, use the arrows to change the channel.
  6. Click OK.
