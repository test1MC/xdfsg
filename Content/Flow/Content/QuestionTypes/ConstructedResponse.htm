Constructed Response

A constructed response question requires participants to build or create an answer.

  1. Open Flow.
  2. Register participants. See Participants on page 1 for more information.
  3. Click Engage question.
  4. Select Constructed Response.
  5. Adjust the question parameters as necessary.
