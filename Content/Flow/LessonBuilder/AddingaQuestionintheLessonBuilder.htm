Adding a Question in the Lesson Builder

You can add a variety of question types in the Lesson Builder, including: True/False, Multiple Choice, Multiple Answer, Short Text, Numeric, Constructed and Informative. Additionally, add text, images, hyperlinks and YouTube videos to your content.

  1. Open the Lesson Builder .
  2. Within the Question Properties section, enter the question parameters using the fields and drop-down menus provided.

  3. Click Set up content.

    The Content editor opens.

    • Click from the left panel to add text to the question.
    • Click from the left panel to add an image to the question. Scroll down below the image to add text.
    • Click from the left panel to add a hyperlinked image to the question. Scroll down below the image to add text.
    • Click from the left panel to add a hyperlink to the question. Scroll down below the image to add text.
    • Click from the left panel to add a link to a YouTube video to the question. Scroll down below the video to add text.
  4. Click Save.
  5. To insert another question, click Add question.
  6. To remove a question, click Remove question.
  7. Repeat steps 2 - 5 as necessary.
  8. Click the Save or Save as icon to save the new lesson.
