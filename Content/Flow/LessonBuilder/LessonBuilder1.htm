Lesson Builder

Lesson Builder installs with Flow as a supplemental application that allows you to create, modify and adapt a Flow lesson that you can display in self-paced mode or presentation mode. When Flow and Lesson Builder install, a shortcut to Lesson Builder is placed on the Desktop (PC)/in the Finder (Mac)/in Dash Home (Linux).
