Uploading Grades to Blackboard 9

  1. Login to Blackboard 9.
  2. Click the Courses tab and select the appropriate course below Course List.
  3. Click Grade Center on the Control Panel.
  4. Click Full Grade Center.
  5. Click the Work Offline drop-down menu and select Upload.
  6. Below Choose File, click Browse... to
    locate the saved file. Choose Comma as the Delimiter Type and click Submit.
  7. Click Submit.

    The assignment now appears in the LMS.
