Turning Technologies® and Study Island

Turning Technologies ResponseCards and receivers can be used with Study Island.

ResponseCards and Study Island

Study Island supports the use of IR, RF, XR and NXT ResponseCards.

Using ResponseCards with Study Island

TurningPoint must be installed but not open prior to using ResponseCards with Study Island. The receiver must also be connected.

  1. Login to Study Island.
  2. Click Programs from the left menu.
  3. Select a grade.
  4. Select a subject.
  5. Place a check next to a topic(s) and click Start Studying.
  6. Click Classroom Response Systems and select the total number of questions from the drop-down menu.
  7. Click Next.
  8. Under the clickers tab, choose TurningPoint as the Default Response System and set the Bandwidth to High. Click the Select Version drop-down menu and choose the type of receiver connected to your computer.

  9. Click Next Tab: Options.
  10. Choose a class from the drop-down menu and click Next Tab: Sessions.
  11. Optionally, enter the Device IDs on the back of the ResponseCards in the Pad ID field next to the appropriate participant and click Next Tab: New Session.
  12. Select New Session or Saved Session and click Start Session.
  13. Once the receiver is connected, click Config Pads to match the Device IDs to participants.
  14. Select Teacher Led or Student Paced mode. For Mac, only Teacher Led mode is available.
  15. After the test appears, click Start Polling.
  16. Click End Session to submit results or Save for Later to finish at another time.
