TurningPoint 5® and Blackboard™ 9

This document outlines the steps for using TurningPoint with the Blackboard 9 Learning Management System (LMS).

How does the integration work?

Participants are instructed to register their response device through the Web Registration Tool hosted by Turning Technologies. The participant information from Blackboard 9 is downloaded and converted into a TurningPoint participant list that is loaded before you begin your polling session. Once the session is run and the results are saved, it can be opened in Results Manager. In Results Manager, the participant list is updated with the latest registrations from the Web Registration Tool. Once the participant list is updated, the session scores are exported from TurningPoint in a specific format and then imported into Blackboard 9.

Downloading the Full Grade Center from Blackboard 9

  1. Login to Blackboard 9.
  2. Click the Courses tab and select the appropriate course below Course List.

  3. Click Grade Center on the Control Panel.
  4. Click Full Grade Center.
  5. Click the Work Offline drop-down menu and select Download.

  6. Below Data, select the radio button labeled User Information Only.
  7. Below Options, choose Comma for Delimiter Type. For Display Hidden Information, select No.
  8. Below Save Location, choose My Computer and click Submit. Do not browse and save to Blackboard 9!
  9. Click Download.
  10. When prompted, choose Save File.

    Now that the CSV file has been saved, it is time to import the information as a TurningPoint participant list.

Uploading Grades to Blackboard 9

  1. Login to Blackboard 9.
  2. Click the Courses tab and select the appropriate course below Course List.
  3. Click Grade Center on the Control Panel.
  4. Click Full Grade Center.
  5. Click the Work Offline drop-down menu and select Upload.
  6. Below Choose File, click Browse... to locate the saved file. Select Comma as the Delimiter Type and click Submit.
  7. Click Submit.

    The assignment now appears in the LMS.
