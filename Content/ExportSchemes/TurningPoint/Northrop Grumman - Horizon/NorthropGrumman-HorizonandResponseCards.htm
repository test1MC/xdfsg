TurningPoint® and Horizon

This document outlines the steps for using Turning Technologies ResponseCards with Horizon.

Technical Requirements

  • Turning Technologies Hardware:
    • ResponseCard NXT
    • ResponseCard XR
    • ResponseCard RF - 02 or 03
    • RRRF-02 Receiver (dark gray)
    • RRRF-01 Receiver (light gray or white) This receiver is not compatible with ResponseCard NXT.

Before Class

  1. Login to your Horizon account.
  2. Create a test and assign it to your students.
  3. Plug the Turning Technologies RF receiver into an available USB port.

Using Turning Technologies ResponseCards with Horizon

  1. Click Test Bank on the Horizon home page.
  2. Enter your search criteria to locate the desired test.
  3. Click Search.
  4. Click the Student Response System icon under Actions.

    If the following message appears, verify that Microsoft® .NET Framework 3.5 or higher is installed on your computer. If it is installed, click OK. If it is not, contact your IT Administrator.

  5. Download and install the application.
  6. Enter your username, Test ID and click Submit.
  7. Click the course associated with the test.
  8. Select the TurningPoint Response System radio button and click Continue.

  9. Follow the steps below Configure Devices to assign devices to participants.

  10. Click Continue to Test after all participants have been assigned a device.
  11. Click Start Polling to begin the test.
    • Participants “click in” to respond to the question.
    • A green checkmark is displayed next to participants who have responded to the question.
  12. Click Next to move to the next question.

Reviewing Responses

Participant responses may be viewed at any time during the test. The Responses for Question graph displays the number of responses for each answer option per question.

 

  1. Click Show Responses to view the Responses for Question graph.

    To hide the Responses for Question graph, click Hide Responses.

  2. Click Review All Questions to view the responses for each test
    question.

    Select the question for which you wish to display participant responses.

Pausing a Test

The test may be paused or stopped at any time.

  1. Click Stop Polling to stop the system from gathering participant responses.
  2. Click Start Polling to resume polling.

Submitting Test Results to Horizon

Data will be immediately available in Horizon reports once the test results are submitted.

  1. Click Submit for Grading to grade and record the test results.
  2. A confirmation window appears. Click Yes to submit the test results to Horizon.
