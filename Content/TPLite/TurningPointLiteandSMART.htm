TurningPoint Lite Plus Add-In

Technical Requirements

  • Microsoft Windows® (32- or 64-bit) 7, 8 or 8.1
  • Mac OS X 10.8, 10.9 or 10.10
  • SMART Notebook 14.1 for Windows or Mac
  • Intel® or AMD 3 GHz processor
  • 1 GB RAM
  • Java™ 7 Runtime Environment or later
  • Standard USB 2.0 port
  • 1024 x 768 at 32-bit color or higher resolution
  • ResponseCard RF LCD, ResponseCard NXT or QT Device
  • RRRF 03 or RRRF 04 receiver

 

  1. Install the TurningPoint Lite Plus Add-in.
    1.  Plug the receiver into an available USB port.
    2. Open the Turning Technologies folder.
    3. Navigate to the TurningPoint Lite Plus - Mac or TurningPoint Lite Plus - PC folder.
    4.  Copy the EXE file (PC)/PKG file (Mac) to the desired save location on your computer.
    5.  Open the file and follow the on-screen instructions.
  2. Open SMART Notebook and click the Add-ons tab and then click Settings .
    1. Select TurningPoint Lite Plus from the left menu.
    2. Click Enable.
    3. Click Done.
  3. Click the Add-ons tab and select TurningPoint Lite Plus.

    It is recommended to uncheck the box labeled Autohide. Unchecking the Autohide box prevents the add-in from floating on top of your questions.

Polling with TurningPoint Lite

  1. Plug the receiver into an available USB port.
  2. To change the channel on the receiver click the drop-down menu next to Channel and select the desired channel.

    When the receiver is plugged in, it is automatically recognized by the computer. The default channel on the receiver is set to 41. Changing the channel allows multiple receivers to accept different sets of responses when in close proximity of one another. The clickers need to be set to the same channel as the receiver when polling.

  3. Click the Question Type drop-down menu to select the question type.
  4. Click to open polling.

    Participants may now respond. As participants respond the number of responses received is displayed.

  5. Click to close polling.
  6. Click the chart icon to view the chart area.
  7. Repeat steps 3 - 6 to poll another question.
