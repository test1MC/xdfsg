TurningPoint Cloud: Steps to Successfully Run Anywhere Polling (Quick Poll)

Plug in Receiver .

Open TurningPoint .

Verify Connection (Receiver and/or ResponseWare).

Click Anywhere Polling.

Click Start to open polling.

Click Stop to close polling.

Save Session.

Edit Session (optional).

Generate Reports (optional).

Instructors must have licensed Turning Accounts in order to view individual reports. Please contact sales@turningtechnologies.com to discuss Turning Account licensing options.
