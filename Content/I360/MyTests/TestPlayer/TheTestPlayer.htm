The Test Player

The Test Player runs self-paced Answer Key or ExamView tests during a class session. During a self-paced test, students answer a list of test questions at their own pace using their own individual student devices.

The Test Player can be accessed from the Insight 360 Cloud Home tab or the My Tests tab.
