Teaching Class

Verify that the Mobi 360 is setup and ready for class by following the steps in Setting Up the Mobi 360 and Clickers or Setting Up the Insight 360 Teacher App and Student Apps. You must also verify that at least one class has be create in Insight 360 Cloud, otherwise you must start an Anonymous session.

Ensure that the student response systems are turned on.

  1. Start a class on the Mobi 360:
    1. Tap Teach Class.
    2. Confirm that the class name, time and period number in the upper left corner are correct.

    If this is not the class you want to start, tap Change Class.

    1. Tap Start Class.
  2. Start a class on the Insight 360 Teacher Application:
    1. Tap the Teacher App icon on the instructor device.
    2. Tap the name of the computer containing your Insight 360 Cloud data as configured on the Teach App page in My Settings.

      If you computer is not listed, tap Connect Directly to a Computer and enter the IP address for your computer. Alternatively, close the application and verify the computer name and password in My Settings.

    3. Enter the password for the computer.

      The Teach a Class screen appears.

    4. Tap the class you want to teach.

      If the class is not listed, tap Start Anonymous Class or create a class in the Insight 360 Cloud software.

    5. Tap Start Class. Student devices can now join the class.
  3. To annotate the screen:
    1. At the bottom of the Mobi 360 screen, tap Lesson.
    2. At the top of the Mobi 360 screen, tap Whiteboard.
    3. Tap Pen.
    4. Write a word or phrase onto the large white writing surface of the Mobi 360.
  4. Asking a CueTag question:
    1. Click Question when the CueTag is displayed on-screen.
    2. Tap Start.

      Ask students to answer the question by pressing the corresponding key on their student response systems.

    3. Tap Stop.
  5. To ask an on the fly question:
    1. Tap Lesson then tap Question.
    2. Tap the box below Type and then tap the desired question type.
    3. Tap the box below Answer and then tap the correct answer depending on the question you want to ask your students.
    4. Tap Back.
    5. Ask you students the question from Step C.
    6. Tap Start.

      Ask students to answer the question by pressing the corresponding key on their student response systems.

    7. Tap Stop.
  6. Assess results:
    1. At the bottom of the Mobi 360 screen, tap Results to view student responses.
    2. Tap View Summary Charts.
  7. To end a session, tap Class and then tap End Class.
