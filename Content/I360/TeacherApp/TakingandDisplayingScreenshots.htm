Taking and Displaying Screenshots

  1. To take a screenshot, tap .

    The screenshot is saved to the Screenshot Sorter.

  2. Tap and then tap to display the Screenshot Sorter.

    The Screenshot Sorter opens on the Insight 360 Teacher app and the computer desktop.

  3. Tap the desired screenshot.

    Alternatively, on the computer desktop, double-click the screenshot to display.

    The screenshot is displayed on the Insight 360 Teacher app and the computer desktop.

  4. Tap to exit Screenshot Mode.
