Creating and Displaying a New Note

Notes are used to temporarily display information on the Insight 360 Teacher app or on the classroom computer screen during a specific class session.

Up to 20 notes may be created during each class session.

  1. Tap and then tap to access the Insight 360 Cloud tools.
  2. Tap to write or draw on the Insight 360 Teacher app screen or writing area.
  3. Tap to remove any mistakes.
  4. Tap to change the background color.
  5. Tap to display the note on the classroom computer screen.
  6. To add another note, tap at the top of the screen.
