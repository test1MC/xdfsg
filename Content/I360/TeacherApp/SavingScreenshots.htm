Saving Screenshots

Screenshots are saved in a folder on the computer’s hard drive. Insight 360 will store a maximum of 10,000 screenshots per session. The total number of screenshots saved is limited by the size of your computer’s hard drive.

    After a session is ended, you can no longer view the screenshots from the Insight 360 Teacher app. However, you can view screenshots for a session through the Session Content Report in Insight 360.

    All screenshots and other Insight 360 information reside on the classroom computer used to set up this data. It will not be accessible if you take the Insight 360 Teacher app to another classroom even if that classroom computer is running Insight 360.
