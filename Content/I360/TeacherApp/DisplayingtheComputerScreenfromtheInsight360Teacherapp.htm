Displaying the Computer Screen from the Insight 360 Teacher app

The Insight 360 Teacher app allows you to control your computer desktop from anywhere in the room.

If your computer is connected to a projector or large monitor, students can also view your computer desktop. This means that you can use Insight 360 Teacher app to present documents, Microsoft PowerPoint presentations, web pages, videos and other media from your computer.
