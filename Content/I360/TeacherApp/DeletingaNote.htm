Deleting a Note

You can delete a note from the computer screen or permanently delete the note from the Insight 360 Teacher app.

  1. To delete a note from the computer screen:
    1. With the Mouse Pointer toolIn Mouse Mode, click X in the top corner of the note.

    Deleting a note from the computer screen does not delete the note from the Insight 360 Teacher app.

  2. To delete a note from the Insight 360 Teacher app:
    1. Tap .
    2. Tap or to find the desired note.
    3. In Note View, tap or at the top of the screen to find the desired note.    
    4. Tap.

    Deleting a note from Note View permanently deletes the note. There is no undo for this action, and the deleted note cannot be retrieved.
