Screenshots

A screenshot is an image of the computer screen at a particular point in a class session. The Screenshot feature saves an image of the classroom computer screen.

Insight 360 automatically saves screenshots during the following actions:

  • Entering/Exiting Multi-User or Constructed Response Mode
  • Starting a Verbal Question
  • Starting a Tagged Question
  • After Editing a Captured Screenshot
