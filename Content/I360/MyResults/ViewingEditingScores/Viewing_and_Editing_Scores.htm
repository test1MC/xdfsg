Viewing and Editing Scores

The Scores section of the My Results tab allows you to access and edit student scores and question responses.

In Scores you can:

  • View scores from a specific session.
  • Edit a student’s responses to individual questions.
  • Adjust a question’s impact on scores.
  • Change a question’s correct answer.
  • View the screenshot associated with a Verbal, Tagged, ExamView, or constructed response question.
