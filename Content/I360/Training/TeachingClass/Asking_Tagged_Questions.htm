Asking Tagged Questions

When a tagged question is visible on your classroom computer screen, the tagged question icon will be active.

  1. Tap the Question Zone icon.

    The Ask a Question screen is displayed.

  2. To set the timer, slide the timer bar to On.
  3. Instruct participants to answer by pressing the corresponding key on their clickers.
  4. Tap Start.

    Tap to display the results on the classroom computer screen.

    Tap to display the timer on the classroom computer screen.

    Tap to display the response grid on the classroom computer screen.
