Using the Scratchpad

The Insight 360 Student app contains a hideaway scratchpad on which you can write or draw.

Use the scratchpad to solve math problems.

Your instructor can view your scratchpad annotations.

  1. Tap Scratchpad.

    The Scratchpad opens.

    • The pen color is black and cannot be changed.
    • The line width is thin and cannot be changed.
    • The eraser width is medium and cannot be changed.
    • Tapping erases everything on the screen.
  2. To close the scratchpad, tap Scratchpad again.
