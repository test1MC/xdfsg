Viewing Results

If your teacher elects to send results to students from Preferences or the Test Player, using the Insight 360 Student app you can view results after an instructor-led question or self-paced exam is completed.

Instructor-Led Question Results

For an instructor-led Verbal, Tagged or ExamView question, the results consist of the submitted answer and the final score (correct or incorrect).

 Self-Paced Test Results

For a self-paced exam, results consist of:

  • Test name
  • Answer sheet display with a list of student’s answers for each question marked either with a check mark (correct) or “X” (incorrect)
  • Number of correct answers out of the total questions
  • Percentage correct (score)

How to view results with the Insight 360 Student app...

  1. Complete an instructor-led question or self-paced test with the Insight 360 Student app.

    The Waiting for Instructor screen appears.   

  2. Keep the Insight 360 Student app open until you see the Results page.
  3. When you are done reading the results, tap CloseBack.
