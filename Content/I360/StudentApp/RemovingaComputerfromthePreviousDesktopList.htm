Removing a Computer from the Previous Desktop List

Once a device running the Insight 360 Student app has successfully joined the class, your teacher's computer information appears at the top of the Previous Desktop list the next time the application is opened.

Removing the class computer from the Connect to Desktop list does not permanently delete the class. The class computer is still displayed in the All Desktops list and can be joined at a later date.

  1. Turn on your device and tap .    
  2. From the Previous DesktopHistory list, press and hold the class computer you want to removetap next to the class computer you want to remove.

    All class computers listed in the upper third of the screen will “wiggle.”

  3. Tap on the left side of the class computer to remove.
