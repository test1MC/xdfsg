Upgrade Devices to Insight 360 Cloud

The Upgrade Devices to Insight 360 Cloud category allows you to upgrade existing CPS, Response or WorkSpace compatible hardware to work with the Insight 360 Cloud software. Follow the on-screen instruction to complete the upgrade process. For detailed instruction on how to upgrade devices, see Upgrading Devices to Insight 360 Cloud.

Upgrading to Insight 360 Cloud is a one way process that cannot be undone. Once the upgrade is complete, your products will no longer connect to CPS, Response or WorkSpace.
