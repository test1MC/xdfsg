Backup/Restore

The Backup/Restore category features a tool that allows you to backup your data.

How to backup data...

  1. Click .

    The Browse for Folder window opens.

  2. Navigate to the desired save location and click OK.
  3. Click Backup.

    The Browse to the Backup/Restore Folder window opens.

  4. Optionally, rename the file.
  5. Click Save.

    A confirmation message notifies you that the backup was successful.

How to restore data...

  1. Click .

    The Browse for Folder window opens.

  2. Navigate to backup save location and click OK.
  3. Click Restore.
