Inactivating a Student

When a student is inactivated, he or she is removed from all classes. However, all student information and class session records are retained in the Insight 360 Cloud database. Inactivating a student prevents the student from appearing in the student list unless you select the Include Inactive Students box.

  2. In the Search box, enter the first or last name of the student to delete.

    Alternatively, select the student to delete from the student list.

  4. Click Edit Student.
  5. Click Inactivate Student.

    A warning appears.

  6. Verify that you really want to inactivate the student and click Yes.
