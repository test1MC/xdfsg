Editing Student Details

Once a student has been added to the Insight 360 Cloud database, you can edit the student's name, ID, grade level and classes. A student can also be enrolled or removed from a class.

  5. Enter the changes in each field as applicable.

    Insight 360 Cloud saves changes to each field automatically when you click away from the edited field.
