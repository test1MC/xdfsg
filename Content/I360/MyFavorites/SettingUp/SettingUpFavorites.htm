Setting Up Favorites

My Favorites is organized into four pages with eight favorites to a page for a total of 32 favorites. The My Favorites left menu shows the names of the pages and how many favorites have been added to each page (indicated by green circles). The main portion of the My Favorites tab allows you to add favorites to the selected page.
