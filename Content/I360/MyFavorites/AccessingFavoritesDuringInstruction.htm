Accessing Favorites During Instruction

Favorites set up in Insight 360 Cloud can be accessed from the Mobi 360 or the Insight 360 Teacher app during instruction. A class must be started to access favorites.

  1. Tap .
  2. Tap the favorite to display.

    The favorite opens on your computer desktop.

  3. To access favorites on other pages:
    1. Tap the down arrow to scroll to the next favorites page.

      On the Insight 360 Teacher app, swipe from right to left to access additional favorites pages.

    1. Tap the up arrow to scroll to the previous favorites page.
