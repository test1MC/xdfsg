Removing a Message from the Message Center

The Message Center on the Insight 360 Cloud Home tab displays important messages and notifications. Most messages require an action to remove the notification.

Reminder to register Insight 360 Cloud

Device Conflict error

Firmware update available

  1. Click the light blue portion of the message.

    The page that requires action opens.

  2. Complete the action to remove the message.

    Click to the right of the message to temporarily remove the message. The message will reappear the next time you restart your computer. To permanently remove the message, complete the corresponding action.
