Downloading and Installing Insight 360 Cloud

Please verify that your computer meets the Technical Requirements before downloading Insight 360 Cloud.

  1. Turn on the classroom computer.
  2. Select the disc that is compatible with your computer’s operating system (Mac or PC) and insert it into the computer. Alternatively, go to http://www.turningtechnologies.com/responsesystemsupport/downloads, click Insight 360 Cloud and select the appropriate download link.
  3. Follow the on-screen installation instructions.
  4. Go to http://www.turningtechnologies.com/responsesystemsupport/downloads.

    If you choose to install Insight 360 Cloud from the DVD, insert the disc and skip to step 7.

  5. Below Polling Software, expand Insight 360 Cloud.
  6. Select the appropriate link (Mac or Windows).
  7. Enter the required information as noted by the asterisks and click Submit.
  8. Click Click to Download.
  9. Open the DMG file (Mac)/EXE file (PC).

    The Insight 360 Cloud Installer opens.

  10. Click Continue (Mac)/Next (PC).
  11. If you agree to the End User License Agreement, click Continue (Mac)/Next (PC).
  12. Click Install.
  13. Click Close (Mac)/Finish (PC).
