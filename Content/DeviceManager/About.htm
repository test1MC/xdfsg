About Device Manager

Device Manager automatically loads and operates in the background of the computer, establishing communication between Turning Technologies hardware and software.

Device Manager is compatible with the following hardware:

Pointer Devices

  • DualBoard
  • iPannel
  • MeetingBoard
  • Mobi Learner
  • MobiKWIK
  • MobiView™
  • SchoolBoard
  • Touch Board
  • Touch Board Plus

Student Response Systems (radio frequency or infrared hubs and their clickers)

  • CPS IR (Gen 1 and Gen 2)
  • CPS Pulse
  • CPS Spark
  • Gen 2 (HID, Wedge and Serial)
  • PRS IR
  • PRS RF

Turning Technologies ResponseCards

  • ResponseCard RF
  • ResponseCard RF LCD
  • ResponseCard NXT

Turning Technologies receivers

  • RRRF-02
  • RRRF-03
  • RRRF-04

Device Manager supports communication via radio frequency (RF), infrared (IR), or USB Connect technology.
