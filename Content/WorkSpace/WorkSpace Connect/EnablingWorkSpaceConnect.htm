Enabling WorkSpace Connect

WorkSpace must be installed on your computer prior to completing this task.

  1. Open WorkSpace.
  2. Click the Main Menu icon and select Preferences.

    The WorkSpace Preferences window opens.

  3. Click the Tablet Settings tab.
  4. Select the box labeled Allow Tablet (Java)/Allow Tablet Connection (PC).

    The Authentication window is displayed.

  5. Enter a name for your computer if it is not already named.
  6. Enter a password for your tablet.
  7. Re-enter the password.
  8. Click Save/Update.

    The application now lists your computer as an available device.

  9. Click OK to exit the Preferences window.
