Device Manager

Use the Device Manager window to manage devices used with WorkSpace. Device Manager detects connected devices and establishes communication with WorkSpace.

The following devices can be used with WorkSpace:

  • DualBoard
  • Touch Board
  • Touch Board Plus
  • MobiView
  • Mobi Learner tablets
  • Mobi 501
  • Clickers
  • WorkSpace Connect

Device Manager runs in the background of your computer to discover, connect and manage devices.
