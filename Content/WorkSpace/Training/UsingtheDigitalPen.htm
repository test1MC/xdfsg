Using the Digital Pen

  1. Rest your hand on the MobiView. Do not touch the digital pen to the MobiView's surface; i.e. hold the digital pen as you would a stylus.
  2. Turn the digital pen's "rocker" buttons away from your fingers.
  3. Single-tap the digital pen to the MobiView. This is equivalent to a left-click with a mouse.

    Double-tap the digital pen to the MobiView. This is equivalent to a double left-click with a mouse.

    Alternatively, use the digital pen's rocker buttons to make left-clicks and double left-clicks.
