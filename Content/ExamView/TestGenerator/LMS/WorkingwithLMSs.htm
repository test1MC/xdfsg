Working with Learning Management Systems

ExamView Assessment Suite works with Learning Management Systems (LMSs) including ANGEL, Blackboard and WebCT. Tests and question banks can either be published directly or exported to a file format that can be uploaded to these LMSs.

Publishing a Test or Question Bank

  1. Open ExamView Test Generator and create or open a test or question bank.
  2. Click File, mouse over Publish To and select the LMS.
  3. Select the LMS server profile and click OK.
  4. Complete the options in the dialog and click OK.
  5. Log in to your LMS to view the ExamView content.

Exporting a Test or Question Bank

  1. Open ExamView Test Generator and create or open a test or question bank.
  2. Click File, mouse over Export and select the LMS.
  3. Name the file and click Save.
  4. Complete the options in the dialog and click OK.
  5. Log in to your LMS and upload the exported test/question bank.
