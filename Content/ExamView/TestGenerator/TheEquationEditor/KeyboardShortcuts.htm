Equation Editor Keyboard Shortcuts

The equation editor in ExamView Test Generator includes numerous shortcut keys to make inserting symbols, inserting templates, adding accents, inserting spaces, and changing font styles as easy as possible. All of the keyboard shortcuts involve the Ctrl key. Templates, style changes, and spaces are inserted by simply pressing Ctrl followed by another key. Symbols are entered by pressing Ctrl+K followed by another key.
