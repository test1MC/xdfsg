The Equation Editor

You can create or edit mathematical equations with ExamView Test Generator. An equation is a series of numbers, mathematical symbols (+, -, ÷ , etc.), and templates. A template is an arrangement of slots in an equation to form a single mathematical unit. Templates may represent fractions, integrals, parentheses groups, square roots, matrices, and many other mathematical structures.

3 + 4 = 7
