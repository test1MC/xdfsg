Checking the Channel Number on the Gen2 RF Clicker

  1. Start with the device turned off.
  2. Press and hold down Power/Join.

    The device and serial numbers are displayed.

  3. While continuing to hold Power/Join down, press and hold down the right arrow (>).
  4. Continue to hold both buttons down for several seconds then release both buttons.
  5. Press the left arrow (<).

    The summary screen appears.

  6. Note the channel number (CH:#). Press Send to return the device to normal function.
