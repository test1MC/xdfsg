Removing a Device

Response devices should only be removed from your Turning Account if you no longer have access to the device. If you remove a device, the device is no longer associated to you in any future sessions.

You may remove a device at the end of the semester because you sold it to the bookstore.

A ResponseWare ID cannot be removed from your Turning Account.

  1. Sign in to your Turning Account.
  2. Select Devices from the left menu.
  3. Select a Device ID from the Device (ResponseCard) ID list.
  4. Click .

    The Confirm Remove window is displayed.

  5. Click Remove.
