Adding Users to a Concurrent License

This feature is for ResponseWare 1.0 Administrators who held a Concurrent license and would like to add users to their new ResponseWare 2.x Concurrent license.

  1. Go to https://account.turningtechnologies.com/.
  2. Sign in to your Turning Account. If you do not have a Turning Account, follow the steps below:
    1. Click Create Account.
    2. Enter your email address and click Next.

      A verification email will be sent to the email address you provide.

    3. Check your email and click the link to verify your Turning Account.

      If you did not receive the verification email, click the Resend Verification button in Turning Account.

    4. Enter all required fields as noted by the asterisks on the Profile page.
    5. From the Role drop-down menu, select Instructor.
    6. Enter and confirm your password in the fields provided.

      The password must be at least eight characters long and contain one lowercase letter, one uppercase letter and one number.

    7. Select the box labeled I accept the Terms of Use and click Create account.

  3. Click Licenses from the left menu.
  4. Click Manage.
  5. Click Add, located at the bottom of the screen.

    The Invite Users window is displayed.

  6. Enter a valid email address in the space provided. Separate multiple email addresses with a comma or semicolon.

    You can copy and paste email addresses from an external document, such as a CSV file. Make certain email addresses are separated by commas or semicolons.

  7. Press Enter.
  8. Click Send.

    The invitees will receive an email from Turning Technologies with instructions on how to create a new ResponseWare account.
