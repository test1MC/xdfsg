Hotspot Interaction

In a hotspot question the participant has to mark one or more areas in a picture. You may choose between single and multiple choice.

  2. Enter a Title in the area provided and select Hotspot Interaction.

  3. Click OK.

    The Edit question screen appears.

  5. Select the type of hotspot interaction.
    • Single Choice - The participant may only select one spot on the image.
    • Multiple Choice - The participant may select more than one spot on the image.
  7. To add the background image, click Choose File, browse to the location of the image file, select the image and click Open.
  8. Click Change to apply the image.

    By default a circle hotspot type is place in the upper left corner of the image.

  9. To adjust the size of the hotspot, click the bottom right corner and drag the mouse to the desired size.
  10. To move the hotspot, place your mouse in the center of the hotspot, click and drag the hotspot to the desired location.
  11. To add another hotspot, click the desired spot type in the Add hotspots area.
  12. To set a correct answer for a single choice hotspot interaction, select the radio button for the desired hotspot from the Hotspots overview at the bottom of the screen. To set a correct answer for a multiple choice hotspot interaction, select the check boxes that correspond to the correct spots.
  13. To remove a hotspot, click the icon to the right of the desired hotspot in the Hotspots overview.
