Text Entry Interaction

The text entry interaction allows the entering of any text that has to be filled in by the participant.

  2. Enter a Title in the area provided and select Text Entry Interaction.

  3. Click OK.

    The Edit question screen appears.

  4. Enter the statement in the area provided.
  5. To add a gap, place the cursor at the desired location and click Add gap. To change a word in your statement into a choice, highlight the word and click Transform text in gap.
  6. In the Solution area, enter the correct answer.
  7. In the Alternatives area, enter alternate versions of the correct answer.
  9. Optionally, check the box to require the answer to be case sensitive.
  10. Optionally enter the value for the tolerance. The tolerance defines how many characters may differ from the correct response. A value of 0 allows no difference.
  11. Enter the number of characters for the gap size.
  12. To remove a gap, place your cursor in front of the [...] and press the delete key on your keyboard.
