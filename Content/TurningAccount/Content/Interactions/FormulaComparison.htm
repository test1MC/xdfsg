Formula Comparison

The formula comparison interaction allows for a formula to be entered as an answer. If enabled, the participant receives formula validation.

  2. Enter a Title in the area provided and select Formula Comparison Interaction.

  3. Click OK.

    The Edit question screen appears.

  4. Enter the statement in the area provided.
  5. To add a gap, place the cursor at the desired location and click Add gap. To change a word in your statement into a choice, highlight the word and click Transform text in gap.
  6. In the Solution area, enter the formula.

    You must use MAXIMA syntax for your input. The answers are checked by formula comparison.

  8. Optionally, check the box to require the answer to be case sensitive.
  9. Select the check formula options.
    • allow (MAXIMA validation)
    • Show syntax errors
    • Show preview (LaTeX rendering
  10. Enter the number of characters for the gap size.
  11. Enter the placeholder text.

    The placeholder text is displayed until the participant enters their own text.

  12. To remove a gap, place your cursor in front of the [...] and press the delete key on your keyboard.
