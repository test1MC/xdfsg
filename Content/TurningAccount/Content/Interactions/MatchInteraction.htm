Match Interaction

A match interaction is made up of response pairs. Each pair contains a source element (the item being matched) and a destination element (the corresponding item). When taking the test, the participant is required to select the source element and drag it to the area next to the destination element. You may also include a wrong answer which will provide the participant with an extra source element that does not have a destination element. When the match interaction is displayed on a test, ExamView Cloud automatically shuffles your source and destination elements.
