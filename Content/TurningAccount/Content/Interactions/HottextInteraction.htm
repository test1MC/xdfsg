Hottext Interaction

The hottext interaction allows you to edit text with a correct and incorrect text element. The participant should recognize and select the appropriate elements.

  2. Enter a Title in the area provided and select Hottext Interaction.

  3. Click OK.

    The Edit question screen appears.

  4. Select the type of hottext interaction.
    • Single Choice - The participant is only required to select one option for the statement.
    • Multiple Choice - The participant may select more than one option for the statement.
  5. Enter the hottext statement in the area provided.
  6. To add a choice, place the cursor at the desired location and click Add choice. To change a word in your statement into a choice, highlight the word and click Convert to choice.
  7. Enter the choice text in the area provided.
  8. To mark this choice as the correct answer, check the box next to Correct.
  9. To remove a hottext item, place your cursor in front of the [...] and press the delete key on your keyboard.
