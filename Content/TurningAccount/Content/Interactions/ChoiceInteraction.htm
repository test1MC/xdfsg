Choice Interaction

The choice interaction lets the participant choose from alternative answers. You may define questions as Single Choice (SC) or Multiple Choice (MC).

  2. Enter a Title in the area provided and select Choice Interaction.

  3. Click OK.

    The Edit question screen appears.

  5. Select the type of choice interaction.
    • Single Choice - The participant may only choose one option from the list of answers.
    • Multiple Choice - The participant may choose more than one option from the list of answers.
  7. Enter the answer text in the area provided.

    Answers may include tables, images, audio or formulas.

  8. Click the New answer area to add an additional answer choice.
  9. Repeat steps 7 and 8 for the remaining answer choices.
  10. To set a correct answer for a single choice question, select the radio button next to the desired answer choice. To set a correct answer for a multiple choice question, select the check box next to each correct answer.
  11. To remove an answer choice, click to the right of the answer.
