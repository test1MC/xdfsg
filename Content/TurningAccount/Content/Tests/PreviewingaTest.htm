Previewing a Test

 

You must have created a test.

  1. From the Content page, click on a test name.

    If a test is open for editing, click Preview in the upper right corner of the screen.

  2. To change the appearance of the preview, click Configure preview.

    You can configure the preview to display, navigation, feedback, correct responses and item titles. Make the necessary adjustments and click Save configuration.

  3. Use the Forward and Back navigation buttons at the bottom of the screen to view your test.
  4. Answer your question and click Save responses to view feedback and correct responses.
  5. Click Finish test when you are done.

    The Test finalized window is displayed.

  6. Click OK.
  7. Click Edit test in the upper right corner.
