Participant Registration

Devices must be registered on Turning Technologies’ hosted web registration site.

  1. Participants go to http://webreg.turningtechnologies.com.

    The Registration Window is displayed.

 

  1. Participants enter:
    1. their First Name, Last Name, User ID, E-mail and Device ID
    2. the Captcha image
  2. Participants then click Register Device.
  3. On the following screen, participants verify the information they have entered is correct and click Final Submission to complete registration, or Cancel to start over.
