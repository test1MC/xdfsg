Moodle™ for TurningPoint® Cloud Participants

You must register your Device ID through Moodle. If you did not register your Device ID through Moodle, you must follow the process below to link your Device ID to your course(s). If you have not yet registered your Device ID, skip to Navigating to the Turning Technologies PaneAccount Registration.

  1. Log into Moodle.
  2. Select the appropriate course from the My Courses menu.
  3. In the Activities section of the course, click the Turning Account Registration link.

    The Turning Account website is displayed.

  4. Sign in with your Turning Account Username and password.

Navigating to the Turning Technologies PaneAccount Registration

All Turning Technologies functions are performed using the ResponseCard Registration linkTurning Account Registration website.

  1. Log into Moodle.
  2. Select the appropriate course from the My Courses menu.

  3. Click ResponseCard Registration below the Turning Technologies pane. In the Activities section of the course, click the Turning Account Registration link.

    The Turning Account Registration website is displayed.

Registering a ResponseCard Device ID

A ResponseCard Device ID must be registered to receive credit in class.

  1. Select ResponseCard.

  2. Enter the six character hexadecimal Device ID located on the back of the ResponseCard below the barcode.
  3. Click Register.

Registering a ResponseWare ID

A ResponseWare ID must be registered to receive credit in class.

  1. Select ResponseWare.

  2. Enter your ResponseWare E-mail Address (Username) and Password.
  3. Click Register.

Editing or Deleting a Device ID

Students can edit or delete Device IDs on the Turning Technologies Device Registration page under the My Registered Devices section.

  1. Click Edit or Delete next to the appropriate Device ID. ResponseWare IDs cannot be edited.
  2. If editing a Device ID, enter the new Device ID in the provided field and click Update.

    If deleting a Device ID, click OK.
