Sakai® for TurningPoint® Cloud Participants

You must register your Device ID through Sakai. If you did not register your Device ID through Sakai, you must follow the process below to link your Device ID to your course(s). If you have not yet registered your Device ID, skip to Navigating to the Turning Technologies Device RegistrationTurning Account Registration Link.

  1. Log into Sakai.
  2. Select the appropriate course tab.
  3. Click the Turning Account Registration link from the left menu.

    The Turning Account website is displayed.

  4. Sign in with your Turning Account Username and password.

Navigating to the Turning Technologies Device RegistrationTurning Account Registration Link

  1. Log into Sakai.
  2. Select the appropriate course tab.
  3. Click the Turning Technologies linkTurning Account Registration link from the left menu.

    The Turning Account website is displayed.

 

 

Registering a ResponseCard Device ID

A ResponseCard Device ID must be registered to receive credit in class.

  1. Select ResponseCard.
  2. Enter the six character hexadecimal Device ID located on the back of the ResponseCard below the barcode.
  3. Click Register.

Registering a ResponseWare ID

A ResponseWare ID must be registered to receive credit in class.

  1. Select ResponseWare.
  2. Enter your ResponseWare Email Address (Username) and Password.
  3. Click Register.

Editing or Deleting a Device ID

Students can edit or delete Device IDs on the Turning Technologies Device Registration page under the ‘My Registered Devices’ section.

  1. Click Edit or Delete next to the appropriate Device ID. ResponseWare IDs cannot be edited.
  2. If editing a Device ID, enter the new Device ID in the provided field and click Update. If deleting a Device ID, click OK.
