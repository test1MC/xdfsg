Canvas® for TurningPoint® Cloud Participants

You must register your Device ID through Canvas. If you did not register your Device ID through Canvas, you must follow the process below to link your Device ID to your course(s). If you have not yet registered your Device ID, skip to .

  1. Log into Canvas.
  2. Select the appropriate course under the Courses & Groups drop-down menu.
  3. Click the Modules link from the left menu.
  4. Click the Turning Account Registration link below Course Modules.

    The Turning Account website is displayed.

  5. Sign in with your Turning Account Username and password.

Registering a Response Device

A ResponseCard or ResponseWare Device ID must be registered to receive credit in class.

  1. Enter your ResponseCard or ResponseWare Device ID in the provided field.
  2. Enter the Captcha in the provided field.
  3. Click Register Device.

    The following window is displayed.

  4. Verify the information that you entered is correct. Click Final Submission to register the Device ID. Click Cancel to start over.
