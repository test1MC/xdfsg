Creating LTI Oauth

Oauth is an open protocol that allows secure authorization in a simple and standard method from various applications.OAuth Community Site. n.d. Web. 10/2/2015.

  1. Sign in to ERP.
  2. Click Manage LTI Information.

    The Manage LTI Information page is displayed.

  3. Click Create in the bottom right corner.

    The Create LTI Oauth page is displayed.

  4. Enter the following information in the corresponding fields:
    • LMS URL
    • LMS Name
    • Organization Name
  5. Click Create.

    The Update LTI Oauth page is displayed.

    You are provided the LTI Oauth Key and LTI Oauth Secret.

    The LTI Oauth Key and LTI Oauth Secret are keys to verify user credentials.

  6. Click Update.

    A notification that the LTI information updated appears in the upper left corner.

  7. Click Cancel to return to the Manage LTI Information page.
