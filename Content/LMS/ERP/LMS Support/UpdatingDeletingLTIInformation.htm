Updating/Deleting LTI Information

  1. Sign in to ERP.
  2. Click Manage LTI Information.

    The Manage LTI Information page is displayed.

  3. Select the LTI that you want to update or delete. For information on searching for LTI information, refer to Searching LTI Information.
  4. Click Update or Delete in the bottom left corner.
  5. To update, click Update, make any necessary adjustments and click Update.
  6. To delete, click Delete, verify that you want to delete the item and click OK.
