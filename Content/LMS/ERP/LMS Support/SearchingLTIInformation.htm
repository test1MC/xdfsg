Searching LTI Information

  1. Sign in to ERP.
  2. Click Manage LTI Information.

    The Manage LTI Information page is displayed.

  3. On the Manage LTI Information page, you can filter LTI information by:
    • ID
    • Organization Name
    • LMS Name
    • LMS URL
    • LTI Oauth Key
    • LTI Oauth Secret

      Click the column header to sort alphabetically.

    Alternatively, use the Search box in the upper right corner to search by ID, Organization Name, LMS Name or LMS URL.
