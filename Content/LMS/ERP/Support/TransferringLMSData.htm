Transferring LMS Data

The Transfer LMS Data feature allows you to assist in transferring a user's LMS data from one email address to another. This is particularly useful if a participant created a Turning Account with a personal email address and needs to transfer the account information to their LMS email address.

  1. Sign in to ERP.
  2. Click Transfer Account Data.
  3. Enter the email address that you want to transfer the data (license) from in the From Email field.
  4. Enter the email address that you want to transfer the data (license) to in the To Email field.
  5. Click Transfer Account Data.
  6. Review the data and click Transfer LMS Data.

    A notification of a successful transfer appears in the upper left corner.
