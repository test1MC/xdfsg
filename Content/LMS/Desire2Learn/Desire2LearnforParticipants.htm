Desire2Learn® for TurningPoint® Cloud Participants

You must register your Device ID through Desire2Learn. If you did not register your Device ID through Desire2Learn, you must follow the process below to link your Device ID to your course(s). If you have not yet registered your Device ID, skip to Navigating to the Turning TechnologiesAccount Registration Link.

  1. Log into Desire2Learn.
  2. Expand the My Courses module and select the appropriate course.
  3. Expand the News module and click the Register Here link.

    The Turning Account website is displayed.

  4. Sign in with your Turning Account Username and password.

Navigating to the Turning TechnologiesAccount Registration Link

  1. Log into Desire2Learn.

  2. Expand the My Courses module and select the appropriate course.
  3. Expand the News module and click the Register ResponseCard HereRegister Here link.

    The Turning Account website is displayed.

Registering a Response Device

A ResponseCard or ResponseWare Device ID must be registered to receive credit in class.

  1. Enter your ResponseCard or ResponseWare Device ID in the provided field.
  2. Enter the Captcha in the provided field.
  3. Click Register Device.

    The following window is displayed.

  4. Verify the information that you entered is correct. Click Final Submission to register the Device ID. Click Cancel to start over.
